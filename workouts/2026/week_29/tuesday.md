# Tuesday (14/07/2026)

**Setup**

- **Equipment**: open floor, pull-up rig, boxes/ab-mats, wall space; optional light DBs
- **Timing**: accumulation 7-min cap; EMOM 12 min; 2× tabata (8 min) + transitions
- **Stimulus**: partner cooperation + strict quality (no sloppy kip); midline and shoulder stamina
- **Scaling A**: reduce reps, preserve strict intent; swap strict HSPU to pike HSPU
- **Scaling B**: incline push-ups + ring rows; hanging knee raises; Z-press to light DBs
- **Logistics**: partners share one lane; keep holds near the rig; pre-assign station order
- **Top Cues**:
  - **Down-ups**: “Chest + thighs touch, stand tall.”
  - **Strict pull-ups**: “Pull ribs to bar.”
  - **Strict HSPU / Z-press**: “Head through, lock out.”

## Warmup (8-10 min)

- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy)**: 2:00 easy bike/row or brisk walk
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate)**: 2 rounds:
  - 6 down-ups
  - 6 ring rows (or band pull-aparts)
  - 10 hollow rocks (or dead bugs)
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard)**: 2 rounds:
  - 6 fast down-ups
  - 10s passive hang + 3 scap pulls

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)

- 30-45s lat stretch each side
- 30-45s wrist extension stretch

## Strength / Skill

### A) Team of 2 — Accumulate

Accumulate **120 down-ups**.

Work/Rest standard: **Work while partner rests** (I-go-you-go).

**Time cap: 7 min (expected: 4-7 min)**

### Reset Window

3 min to set up for the EMOM (choose pull-up + dip + core scales).

## Main Conditioning

### B) 12' EMOM

1. 5 **Strict pull-ups**
2. 15 **Dips**
3. 16 **Alt one-leg T2B** (or hanging knee raise)
4. 5 **Strict HSPU** / **10 Z-press**

**Target**: finish each minute with ~15-25s to reset.

### C) 2× Tabata (4:00 each)

Rotate through:

1. **Chin-up bar hold** (top or active hang)
2. **Bottom pull-ups** (dead hang + first inch pull)
3. **L-sit hold** (parallettes/boxes)
4. **Bottom push-ups** (paused just off the floor)

**Target**: holds are high-quality; shake out shoulders between intervals.

**Scaling options**

- **Scaling A**: banded strict pull-ups, box/bench dips, hanging knee raises, pike HSPU, light DB Z-press
- **Scaling B**: ring rows, incline push-ups, plank holds instead of L-sit, dead bug instead of hang work

## Cooldown & Debrief

- 60-90s easy walk + breathing
- 30-45s pec stretch each side
- Quick debrief: strict reps only; keep core tight on hangs
