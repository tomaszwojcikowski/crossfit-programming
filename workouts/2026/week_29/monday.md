# Monday (13/07/2026)

**Setup**

- **Equipment**: rack + barbell + plates (squat), 1 light bar/DBs for STO, jump ropes
- **Timing**: 60-min class cap; strength ~18-20 min; metcon 12-min cap
- **Stimulus**: heavy-ish squat volume, then a moderate-paced triplet that stays unbroken/smooth
- **Scaling A**: back squat by feel at RPE 7-8; reverse lunges lighter; STO to DB push press
- **Scaling B**: goblet squat or tempo air squat; step-back lunges + light DB push press; single-unders
- **Logistics**: pair athletes by rack; keep a separate “conditioning bar” or DB option ready
- **Top Cues**:
  - **Back squat**: “Drive the floor away.”
  - **Lunge**: “Long step, back knee soft touch.”
  - **STO**: “Dip straight, punch up.”

## Warmup (8-10 min)

- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy)**: 2:00 easy bike/row + nasal breathing
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate)**: 2 rounds:
  - 6 air squats
  - 6 alternating reverse lunges
  - 6 push-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard)**: 2 rounds:
  - 10 fast single-unders (or 6 DU practice)
  - 5 jump squats

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)

- 30-45s calf stretch (each side)
- 30-45s front rack stretch (bar/rack or doorway)

- 6 alternating reverse lunges (bodyweight) → then 6 with load
- 6 shoulder-to-overhead with empty bar/DBs
- 15-20 single-unders (or 6-10 DU)

## Main Conditioning

- 12 alternating **OH reverse lunges** (40/25 kg)

- 36 **Double-unders**

**Scaling options**
