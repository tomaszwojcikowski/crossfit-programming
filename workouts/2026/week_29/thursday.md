# Thursday (16/07/2026)

**Setup**

- **Equipment**: rowers, AABs, jump ropes, med balls, DB/KBs, cones (8m), floor space
- **Timing**: 10× (2:00 work / 1:00 rest) = 30:00 total
- **Stimulus**: repeatable hard efforts; keep transitions fast and recover on the 1:00 off
- **Scaling A**: lower cal/reps, keep same station order + time
- **Scaling B**: swap machine calories to distance/time; low-impact jumping option
- **Logistics**: 5 station lanes; athletes rotate in order; pair people to share equipment smoothly
- **Top Cues**:
  - **Machines**: “Smooth strokes, don’t sprint-die.”
  - **Box jumps**: “Step down, stay tall.”
  - **Shuttle**: “Touch-and-go, turn tight.”

## Warmup (8-10 min)

- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy)**: 2:00 easy machine (row/bike)
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate)**: 2 rounds:
  - 10 jumping jacks
  - 6 inchworms
  - 10 air squats
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard)**: 2 rounds:
  - 20s fast single-unders
  - 3 burpees

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)

- 30-45s ankle rocks each side
- 30-45s thoracic opener (on floor)

## Movement Prep

Quick station rehearsal (1 round):

- 5 hard row strokes + 3 burpees
- 10 box jump overs or step-ups
- 10 double-under attempts (or 20 SU)
- 2 shuttle lengths (8m down + back)
- 6 med-ball over-shoulder reps (light)

## Main Conditioning

### 10 Rounds — 2:00 ON / 1:00 OFF

Rotate stations A→E.

**Target**: keep work steady for the full 2:00, then recover in the 1:00 off.

#### A) Row

20/15 cal row, then **burpees** for remaining time.

#### B) AAB

15/12 cal AAB, then **box jumps** (60/50 cm) for remaining time.

#### C) Jump Rope

60 double-unders, then **OH walking** (2×KB/DB) for remaining time.

#### D) Shuttle

20 shuttle lengths (1 = 8m), then **med ball over the shoulders** for remaining time.

#### E) Bodyweight

60 mountain climbers, then **plank** for remaining time.

**Target**: finish the “buy-in” in ~0:45-1:15 so there’s time for the second movement.

**Scaling options**

- **Scaling A**: 16/12 row cals; 12/9 AAB cals; 90 single-unders; lighter OH walk; 16 shuttle lengths; 40 mountain climbers
- **Scaling B**: 1:00 row hard + 1:00 burpees; 1:00 bike hard + 1:00 step-ups; 2:00 fast singles; farmer carry instead of OH; 2:00 brisk walk instead of shuttle; dead bug instead of plank

## Cooldown & Debrief

- 2:00 easy walk + breathing
- 30-45s pec stretch each side
- Debrief: consistent pacing wins; transitions are the hidden work
