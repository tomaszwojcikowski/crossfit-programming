# Friday (17/07/2026)

**Setup**

- **Equipment**: barbell + plates (DL + C&J + complex), pull-up rig, ab-mats
- **Timing**: 25-min AMRAP; brief primer + clear partner rules
- **Stimulus**: team pacing + barbell cycling under fatigue; keep sets short and repeatable
- **Scaling A**: reduce barbell load and/or gymnastics reps; keep “I-go-you-go full round”
- **Scaling B**: pull-ups instead of BMU; hanging knee raises; power clean + push press
- **Logistics**: one bar per team; teams claim a rig lane; define “full round” standard before start
- **Top Cues**:
  - **Barbell**: “Fast feet, quiet catch.”
  - **Gymnastics**: “Short sets, quick swap.”
  - **Burpees**: “Step up, breathe.”

## Warmup (8-10 min)

- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy)**: 2:00 easy row/bike
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate)**: 2 rounds:
  - 6 down-ups
  - 6 empty-bar deadlifts
  - 6 kip swings (or scap pulls)
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard)**: 2 rounds:
  - 3 burpees
  - 3 empty-bar hang power cleans

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)

- 30-45s front rack stretch
- 30-45s triceps/lat stretch

## Movement Prep

Barbell ramp (keep crisp):

- 6 deadlifts (empty bar)
- 4 hang power cleans
- 2 front squats
- 2 push jerks

Then 1 short partner practice:

- 1 round each: 2 pull-ups + 2 T2B (or scale) + 2 burpees over bar

## Main Conditioning

### Team of 2 — 25' AMRAP

**Standard**: **10 rounds I-go-you-go, full round each** (partner completes the whole round before switching).

#### Block 1 — 10 rounds

- 4 **BMU** / 8 **pull-ups**
- 8 **deadlifts** (80/50 kg)
- 4 **burpees over the bar**

#### Block 2 — 10 rounds

- 4 **clean & jerks** (70/35 kg)
- 8 **T2B**
- 4 **burpees over the bar**

#### Remaining time

**I-go-you-go barbell complex**:

2 deadlifts + 2 hang cleans + 2 front squats + 2 jerks

**Expected rounds**: most teams complete part/all of Block 1 and start Block 2; strong teams reach the complex.

**Scaling options**

- **Scaling A**: pull-ups only (or C2B), DL 60/40, C&J 50/30, hanging knee raises, burpees step-over
- **Scaling B**: ring rows, DB deadlifts, DB power clean + push press, sit-ups, burpee to plate

## Cooldown & Debrief

- 2:00 easy walk
- 30-45s hamstring stretch each side
- Debrief: keep rounds unbroken but not maximal; switch immediately after each full round
