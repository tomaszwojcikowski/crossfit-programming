# Thursday (30/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Single dumbbell (22.5/15 kg), box (60/50 cm), open floor lane for partner burpees
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Long team grinder. The first two I-go-you-go rounds teach partner rhythm, then the larger chipper rewards clean handoffs and steady DB breathing.
- Scaling A: DB to 15/10 kg. Box to 50/40 cm. Burpee over partner to down-up beside partner. Keep the rep structure the same.
- Scaling B: Step-overs stay low and controlled. Alternating DB snatches to Russian swings or high pulls if overhead is limited. Hang squat cleans to DB front squats.
- Logistics: One lane per team with DB and box staged together. On burpee-over-partner reps, the non-working partner holds a strong plank only while the other athlete jumps. Switch immediately after each set of 10.
- Top Cues: "Switch fast, not frantic." "Step down and keep moving." "Meet the dumbbell in the front rack."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog or bike flush
  - 8 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Bootstrappers
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Step-over rhythm build on a low target or line
  - 6 x Alternating reverse lunges
  - 6 x High plank shoulder taps/side
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast feet into burpee pop-up
  - 4 x Alternating DB snatches with light load
  - 4 x Box step-overs
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Hip flexor stretch: 45s/side
- T-spine opener on floor: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 4 x Alternating DB snatches
- 4 x Box step-overs with the DB
- 2 x Burpees over a planking partner
- 4 x DB hang squat cleans

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 - 26' Window
**Part A - 2 rounds, I go-you go by exercise:**

- 10 Alternating DB Snatches (22.5/15 kg)
- 10 Box Step-Overs with DB
- 10 Burpees Over Partner (partner in plank)
- 10 DB Hang Squat Cleans

Each athlete completes all 10 reps of the current exercise before switching to the partner. After both athletes finish the exercise, move to the next station.

**Then, for time:**

- 100 Alternating DB Snatches
- 80 Box Step-Overs with DB
- 60 Burpees Over Partner

**In the remaining time:**

- Max DB Hang Squat Cleans

**Target:** fit teams should finish the chipper with 3-6 minutes left and accumulate 40-80 hang squat cleans. Average teams should get deep into the burpee block and still leave time for a short clean sprint.

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Pigeon stretch: 45s/side
3. Forearm stretch: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "We used the first two rounds to lock in our handoff plan before the big rep blocks started."
