# Tuesday (28/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rack, barbell, plates, box (60/50 cm)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Peak back squat loading first, then repeatable gymnastic and jumping density with one scoring barbell minute each cycle.
- Scaling A: Keep squat percentages if possible. HSPU to 8-10 box pike push-ups. Box jump overs to 20 reps. Shoulder-to-overhead at 40/20 kg.
- Scaling B: Reduce squat percentages by 5-10%. HSPU to hand-release push-ups. Box step-overs 20 reps. Push press at 30/20 kg in minute 3.
- Logistics: One rack and one box per athlete. Stage the box beside the rack before squats start so the class can transition directly into the EMOM.
- Top Cues: "Drive the floor away." "Rebound and go." "Punch the bar to lockout."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy bike flush or jog
  - 8 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Air squats
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Quick-feet shuffle or line hops
  - 6 x Pike push-ups
  - 6 x Empty-bar strict press
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast box step-up cadence
  - 4 x Jump squats
  - 3 x Push press with empty bar
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Couch stretch: 45s/side
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side

## Strength: Back Squat (14 min)
**Every 2:00 for 7 sets:**

- 6 @ 80%
- 5 @ 85%
- 4 @ 87%
- 3 @ 90%
- 2 @ 93%
- 2 @ 93%
- 1 @ 96%

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Strip the rack to the EMOM load, confirm HSPU scale, and move the box tight to each lane.

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 3 x Handstand push-ups or scale
- 6 x Box jump overs
- 3 x Shoulder-to-overhead @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: 16' EMOM

Board note: the source lists four stations plus a rest minute, so this is programmed as a 4-minute cycle for 4 rounds.

- **Minute 1:** 12 Handstand Push-Ups
- **Minute 2:** 24 Box Jump Overs (60/50 cm)
- **Minute 3:** Max Shoulder-to-Overhead (50/25 kg)
- **Minute 4:** Rest

**Target:** finish minutes 1-2 in ~30-50s, then use the shoulder-to-overhead minute as the scoring minute without losing bar path. The rest minute should be full recovery.

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30s/side
3. Child's pose: 45s
4. Bright spot: "I hit the squat numbers and still kept clean lockouts in minute 3."
