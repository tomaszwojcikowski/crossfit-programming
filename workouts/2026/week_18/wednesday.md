# Wednesday (29/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, jump rope, dumbbells (2x22.5/15 kg), AbMat or mat
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Short, repeatable interval work. Win each minute by clearing the buy-in fast enough to earn calm double-under volume, not by redlining the first 20 seconds.
- Scaling A: Bar muscle-ups to 3 bar muscle-ups or 5 chest-to-bar pull-ups. Toes-to-bar to 10 hanging knee raises. Devil press load to 2x15/10 kg. Double unders 30-40 single unders.
- Scaling B: Jumping chest-to-bar pull-ups or banded pull-ups for the first station. Knee raises to V-ups if hanging volume is limited. Devil press load to 2x10/7.5 kg. Single unders only.
- Logistics: Split the class evenly across rig lanes. Pair each lane with one rope and one DB set so athletes rotate only inside their own station footprint.
- Top Cues: "Earn the rope." "Snap to hollow." "Keep the dumbbells close on the turnover."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jump rope or jog
  - 8 x Scap pull-ups
  - 8 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Rope rhythm build or line hops
  - 6 x Kip swings
  - 6 x V-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast rope cadence, easy reset between
  - 2 x Bar muscle-up transition drills or chest-to-bar singles
  - 3 x Devil press with light DBs
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 2 x Bar muscle-ups or scale
- 4 x Toes-to-bar or scale
- 2 x Devil presses @ workout load
- 20 x Double unders or 30 x single unders

## Main Conditioning: Intervals
**12 x 60s on / 60s off, alternating A and B for 6 rounds each:**

- **A:** 5 Bar Muscle-Ups, 10 Toes-to-Bar, then max Double Unders in the remaining time
- **B:** 5 Devil Presses (2x22.5/15 kg), 10 V-Ups, then max Double Unders in the remaining time

Use each off minute as full recovery before the next interval starts.

**Target:** clear the buy-in in ~20-35s and keep the remaining rope work smooth enough that the sixth round looks like the first.

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Puppy pose: 45s
3. Figure-4 glute stretch: 45s/side
4. Bright spot: "I did not panic the rope after the gymnastics buy-in."
