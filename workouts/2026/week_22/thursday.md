# Thursday (28/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Kettlebell, jump rope, dumbbell, box, target, pull-up rig
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Repeating 2-minute windows where athletes complete a fixed buy-in, then accumulate skill or grip-biased work with the time remaining. The goal is sustainable output across all 3 sets.
- Scaling A: Russian swings at 24/16 kg. Double-unders to 40 single-unders. Dumbbell snatches at 17.5/10 kg. Box step-overs without load. Burpee-to-target to standard burpees. Dead hang broken as needed.
- Scaling B: Russian swings at a lighter bell and reduce to 24 reps. Dumbbell snatches to 16 reps. Step-overs to bodyweight. Burpee buy-in to 12 reps.
- Logistics: Set all five stations in one lane before class. Athletes should know exactly where the rope, box, target, and pull-up bar are before the first 2-minute clock starts.
- Top Cues: "Finish the buy-in, then settle." "Switch hands with intent." "Hang long, not tense."

## Warmup (8 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy rope skip, jog, bike, or shuttle runs
  - 20s Active hang
  - 6 x Bodyweight good mornings
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Double-under timing practice or pogo hops
  - 6 x Box step-overs bodyweight
  - 10 x Fast rope turns
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast rope cadence
  - 2 x Burpees to target
  - 10s Dead hang
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
- Hamstring fold: 45s

## Movement Prep (6 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 light Russian swings
  - 6 alternating dumbbell snatches light total
  - 4 box step-overs with the dumbbell
  - 10 double-unders or 20 single-unders
  - 10s active hang

Move the second round faster than the first so athletes feel the 2-minute window pace.

## Main Conditioning: 3 Sets
Each set includes:

- 2' AMRAP:
  - 30 Russian Swings (32/24 kg)
  - Then max Double Unders in remaining time
- 1' Rest
- 2' AMRAP:
  - 20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatches (22.5/15 kg)
  - Then max Box Step-Overs with dumbbell in remaining time
- 1' Rest
- 2' AMRAP:
  - 15 Burpees to target
  - Then max Dead Hang in remaining time
- 1' Rest before the next set

**Target:** athletes should consistently finish each buy-in with 30-60s left to accumulate reps or hang time.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
3. Hamstring fold: 45s
