# Friday (29/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Open run lane, pull-up rig, open floor
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Partner piece modeled on Murph pacing, with shared runs around upper-body volume and synchronized squat work. Teams should stay well clear of failure on pull-ups and push-ups.
- Scaling A: Shorten each lap to 200m or shuttle equivalent. Pull-ups to banded pull-ups or ring rows. Push-ups to elevated push-ups. Synchro air squats stay together.
- Scaling B: Reduce the in-round volume to 3 sets instead of 4. Pull-ups to 4 reps each turn and push-ups to 8 reps each turn.
- Logistics: Partners run together, then complete 4 in-gym sets each round. The first round skips the run, so brief that clearly before the start.
- Top Cues: "Run smooth, not fast." "Break before you fail." "Match the squat tempo."

## Warmup (8 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, shuttle runs, or bike
  - 6 x Scap pull-ups
  - 8 x Air squats
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle build-ups or quick feet in place
  - 5 x Push-ups
  - 6 x Alternating reverse lunges total
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s run acceleration or fast high-knee burst
  - 2 x Pull-ups or scaled version
  - 8 x Fast air squats
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Calf stretch on wall: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (6 min)
- 1 practice round:
  - 100m run together
  - Partner A: 3 pull-ups
  - Partner B: 3 pull-ups
  - Partner A: 6 push-ups
  - Partner B: 6 push-ups
  - 10 synchro air squats together
- Then 1 more in-place primer:
  - 2 pull-ups each
  - 4 push-ups each
  - 6 synchro air squats

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 - "As If Murph"
- Buy-in: 3 Laps run together
- Then 5 Rounds For Time:
  - Round 1: no run
  - Rounds 2-5: 1 Lap run together
  - Then 4 sets of:
    - 5 Pull-Ups each, "I go, you go"
    - 10 Push-Ups each, "I go, you go"
    - 15 Synchro Air Squats
- Buy-out: 3 Laps run together

**Time cap:** 37 min (expected: 28-37 min)

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Calf stretch on wall: 45s/side
