# Monday (25/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rack, barbell, plates, machine or shuttle option, jump rope, dumbbells
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Heavy front squat volume first, then a fast alternating interval piece that flips between leg-heavy machine work and rope-plus-dumbbell turnover.
- Scaling A: Work to 5x6 front squats at RPE 7-8 if the board percentage is too high. Machine calories down to 10/8. Double-unders to 60 single-unders. Devil press at 2x20/12.5 kg.
- Scaling B: Reduce the strength work to 4x6. Front squats in the metcon to 40/25 kg. Double-unders to penguin taps or line hops. Devil press to 6 reps.
- Logistics: Keep one bar, one machine or shuttle lane, one rope, and one dumbbell pair per athlete. The alternating 2-minute format only works if all stations are staged before the strength piece ends.
- Top Cues: "Stay tall in the rack." "Push hard, then settle fast." "Keep the dumbbells close."

## Warmup (8 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy bike, row, jog, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Air squats
  - 20 x Rope hops
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Machine build or shuttle rhythm
  - 6 x Squat-to-stand
  - 20 x Fast rope hops
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s machine surge or fast shuttle accelerations
  - 4 x Jump squats
  - 10 x Fast line hops
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Calf stretch on wall: 45s/side

## Strength A: Front Squat (15 min)
**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:**
- 6 Front Squats

Build with intent before set 1:
- Empty bar x5
- ~40% x5
- ~55% x4
- ~65% x3
- ~72% x2

Source note preserved: the board shows **5x6 @ 80% 1RM + 2 kg from previous week**.

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Set the bar to workout load, place the dumbbells, and clear the rope lane.
- Then 1 quick primer:
  - 10 easy rope turns
  - 2 light devil presses
  - 2 workout-load front squats

## Main Conditioning: Every 2:00 for 8 Sets
Alternate between:

- Odd sets:
  - 12/10 Calories on machine
  - 10 Front Squats (50/30 kg)
- Even sets:
  - 30 Double Unders
  - 8 Devil Presses (2x22.5/15 kg)

**Target:** finish each set with about 20-35s left to reset. If athletes are missing that window, scale the rope or dumbbell volume first.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Figure-4 glute stretch: 45s/side
