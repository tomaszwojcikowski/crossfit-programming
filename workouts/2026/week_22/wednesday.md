# Wednesday (27/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, wall space, medball, open floor lane
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Skill-forward EMOM that rotates between gymnastics tension, inversion control, loaded carry breathing, and rest. Quality should stay high across all 6 rounds.
- Scaling A: Wall walks to 2-3 reps. Toes-to-bar to hanging knee raises. Handstand walk to 2x5m bear crawl or wall-facing shoulder taps. Medball to 40/20 kg.
- Scaling B: Wall walks to pike walk-ins. Toes-to-bar to V-ups. Handstand walk to crab walk or bear crawl. Bear-hug carry with a lighter sandbag or medball.
- Logistics: Keep one lane per athlete with wall, rig, and carry path close together. The rest station matters here, so do not let athletes drift into extra practice between minutes.
- Top Cues: "Make each rep look the same." "Own the line before you move." "Breathe behind the ball."

## Warmup (8 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy row, bike, jog, or shuttle runs
  - 20s Hollow hold
  - 20s Arch hold
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle build-ups or moderate machine cadence
  - 4 x Scap pull-ups
  - 20s Box plank shoulder taps
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s short machine surge or fast shuttle acceleration
  - 1 x Wall walk
  - 5m Bear crawl or handstand walk scale
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 30-45s

## Movement Prep (6 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 1 wall walk
  - 4 hanging knee raises or toes-to-bar
  - 5m handstand walk attempt or scale
  - 10m medball bear-hug carry

Round 2 should look sharper than round 1, not more fatiguing.

## Main Conditioning: 30' EMOM
1. 4 Wall Walks
2. 12 Toes-to-Bar
3. 2 x 5m Handstand Walk
4. Max meters Walking Medball Bear-Hug (50/30 kg)
5. Rest

**Target:** the first three stations should leave 15-25s to reset. Station 4 is the push station, but posture and breathing should stay under control.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Wrist stretch on floor: 30-45s/side
