# Friday (22/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Jump ropes, kettlebell, open floor
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team-of-2 rope buy-in followed by a long partner AMRAP built around kettlebell swings, down-ups, push press, and synchronized sit-ups.
- Scaling A: Double-unders to single-unders. Kettlebell swings at 20/12 kg. Push press at a lighter bell or reduce to 75 reps. Synchro sit-ups to regular sit-ups if needed.
- Scaling B: Cut the buy-in to 150 double-unders or 300 single-unders. Reduce the AMRAP reps by 25%. Use a lighter kettlebell and keep all reps crisp.
- Logistics: Stage ropes and kettlebells before class. Partners should agree on switch points for the AMRAP so the work stays organized instead of chaotic.
- Top Cues: "Keep the rope moving." "Hips drive the bell." "Switch fast, not frantic."

## Warmup (6 min)
- 6:00 general warmup: easy jog, shuttle runs, or rope skip rhythm
- Then 20 single-unders, 10 light swings, and 10 sit-ups before the buy-in

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 - 26' Window
- Buy-in: 200 Double Unders / 450 Single Unders
- Then AMRAP:
  - 100 Kettlebell Swings (24/16 kg)
  - 50 Down-Ups
  - 100 Push Press (1 KB)
  - 50 Synchro Sit-Ups

Split the AMRAP work any way between partners unless the movement demands synchro work.

**Expected rounds:** 1-2 (fit: 2, ~11:00-14:00/round; average: 1-2, ~13:00-16:00/round)

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Hamstring stretch: 45s/side
3. Child's pose: 45s
