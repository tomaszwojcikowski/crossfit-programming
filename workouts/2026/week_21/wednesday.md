# Wednesday (20/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates, pull-up rig, open floor
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Snatch-position practice first, then a short interval piece that pairs unbroken hang snatches with down-ups and pull-ups.
- Scaling A: Use power snatch + hang power snatch if the receiving position is not stable yet. Reduce the interval load to 30/20 kg if needed. Pull-ups to banded pull-ups or jumping pull-ups.
- Scaling B: Snatch pulls + muscle snatches for the first piece. Hang snatch to 25/15 kg. Pull-ups to ring rows. Down-ups to step-back burpees.
- Logistics: Set the bar once for the first piece, then strip and reload it before the 2:30 intervals. Keep the rig lane next to the bar lane.
- Top Cues: "Punch under the bar." "Stand the snatch up before the next rep." "Breathe, then move."

## Warmup (6 min)
- 6:00 general warmup: easy row, bike, jog, or shuttle runs
- Then 5 PVC pass-throughs, 5 air squats, and 5 snatch-grip hinge reps

## Strength/Skill A: Snatch Complex
**Every 1:30 for 9 sets:**
- Sets 1-3: 2 High Hang Snatches + 2 Overhead Squats
- Sets 4-6: 2 Hang Snatches + 2 Overhead Squats
- Sets 7-9: 2 Low Hang Snatches + 2 Overhead Squats

Load: light/moderate, about 60-70%.

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Change the bar to the interval load and move to the rig lane.

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 2 Hang Snatches at workout load
- 5 Down-Ups
- 3 Pull-Ups or scaled version

## Main Conditioning: Every 2:30 for 4 Sets
- 10 Hang Snatches (unbroken) at 40/25 kg
- 5 Down-Ups if the snatches break
- 10 Pull-Ups

**Target:** finish each set in about 1:45-2:00 and keep 30-45s to reset.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Deep squat hold: 45s
