# WEDNESDAY: Snatch EMOM & 1RM

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Barbell, Plates, (Lifting shoes)
*   **Stimulus:** High-skill technical volume followed by heavy single.
*   **Standards:** Full squat (hip crease below knee). Locked out overhead.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Complexity → Load → Volume
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** As written (full snatch)
    *   **B:** Power Snatch + Overhead Squat (if limited depth)
    *   **C:** Snatch Pulls + Snatch Balance practice (focus on speed)
*   **Top Cues:** "Punch the bar up." "Sit tall." "Fast feet."
*   **Bottleneck:** Barbell space. No shared bars for 1RM.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** Row/Bike, 10 Pass-throughs, 10 Overhead squats (PVC)
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** Row/Bike, 10 Snatch grip DL, 10 Muscle snatches (PVC/Empty bar)
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** Row/Bike, 5 Snatch balances, 5 Squat snatches (Empty bar)
*   *Cues:* "Active shoulders." "Bar close."

## Mobility (60-90s)

*   Thoracic Extension (roller): 45s
*   Wrist Mobility: 30s
*   Ankle stretch (knee to wall): 30s/side

---

## Workout

**A) 16' EMOM (Technical Build)**
*   Minute 1: 2 Squat Snatches
*   Minute 2: 2 Hang Squat Snatches
*   Minute 3: 1 Hang Squat Snatch + 2 OHS
*   Minute 4: 1 Hang Squat Snatch + 4 Overhead Lunges
*   *Repeat 4 times. Increase weight slightly each round if movement remains crisp.*

**[ Reset Window: 3:00 ]**
*   *Action:* Quick water. Add weight for 1RM attempt.
*   *Mental:* Focus on one cue from the EMOM.

**B) Find 1RM Snatch (Tc: 8:00)**
*   Build to a heavy technical single.
*   *Goal:* Clean movement, no misses.

---

## Cooldown (5-7 min)

**Protocol: Neural Reset (Post-Olympic Lifting)**

*   **Spinal Decompression (60s)**
    *   Dead hang from pull-up bar (use straps if needed).

*   **Walk & Breathe (2 min)**
    *   Easy walk, nasal breathing. Box breathing pattern (4-4-4-4).

*   **Mobility (3-4 min):**
    *   **Thoracic Extension:** 90s. Foam roller mid-back, arms overhead.
    *   **Child's Pose with Side Reach:** 60s/side. Walk hands to each side.

*   **Debrief (during mobility):**
    *   *Bright Spot:* "Heavy single technically clean?"
    *   *Takeaway:* "Stay patient" on the pull.
