# Tuesday (05/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, bench or floor space, pull-up rig, matching DBs/KBs, paralettes
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Upper-body strength endurance. Four push-pull superset blocks—move continuously within each round, rest only between rounds.
- Scaling A: Bench press at moderate load (~RPE 7). DB press at comfortable weight. Banded strict pull-ups. All other bodyweight movements as written.
- Scaling B: Floor press instead of bench press. Banded pull-ups. Incline or knee push-ups for paralette push-ups. Reduce DB/KB weight throughout.
- Logistics: Bench or floor space and barbell per athlete, plus two matching DBs or KBs. Paralettes staged beforehand. Pull-up rig lanes assigned before class.
- Top Cues: "Control the descent." "Pull to your sternum." "Squeeze at the top."

## Warmup (8 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy bike flush or jog
  - 8 x Band pull-aparts
  - 8 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Quick-feet pattern or shuttle jog
  - 6 x Scap pull-ups
  - 6 x Pike push-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast push-up cadence
  - 3 x Strict pull-ups or banded
  - 4 x Empty-bar strict press
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (2 min)
- 5 x Bench press or floor press @ working load
- 5 x Strict pull-ups or scale

## Main Conditioning: 4-Block Strength Circuit

**\* Rest 2:00 between rounds within each block. No rest between exercises within a round.
Complete all 3 rounds of each block before moving to the next.**

**A. 3 Rounds:**
- 15 Bench Press / Floor Press
- 10 Strict Pull-Ups

**B. 3 Rounds:**
- 10 DB/KB Strict Press (2x DB/KB)
- 10 Australian Pull-Ups (ring rows)

**C. 3 Rounds:**
- 10 Paralette Push-Ups
- 15 Bent Over Row (2x DB/KB)

**D. 3 Rounds:**
- 15 Biceps Curls
- 15 Triceps Extension

**Target:** ~39-42 min total work time. Keep each superset under 3:00 per round to stay within the 60-min cap.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Puppy pose: 45s
3. Thread needle: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "I moved steadily through all four blocks without the rest windows bleeding long."
