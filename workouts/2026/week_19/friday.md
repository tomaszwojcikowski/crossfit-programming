# Friday (08/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell + plates, kettlebell 24/16 kg, open OTB lane (one per team)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Three-part sprint ladder for teams. Parts A and B are fast-capped sprints that teach the effort ceiling; Part C repeats all four movements under a longer cap and rewards pacing consistency.
- Scaling A: Thrusters to 35/20 kg. Burpees to down-ups over the bar. KB snatches to 20/12 kg. Front rack lunges to 35/20 kg. Keep rep counts.
- Scaling B: Thrusters to 30/15 kg. Down-ups beside the bar. Alternating Russian KB swings 24/16 kg instead of snatches. DB front rack lunges.
- Logistics: One barbell + one KB per team, staged together. Mark the OTB lane before class. Confirm the 2-min rest window is timed by the coach so all teams start Part C together.
- Top Cues: "Stand all the way up." "Jump over, not around." "Snap the hip at the top."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog or machine flush
  - 8 x Leg swings/side
  - 8 x Hip circles
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle run build or directional hops
  - 6 x Alternating reverse lunges
  - 6 x Goblet squats with light KB
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast thruster cadence with PVC or empty bar
  - 3 x Empty-bar thrusters
  - 3 x KB snatches light
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Hip flexor stretch: 45s/side
- Ankle rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 3 x Thrusters @ workout load
- 3 x Front rack lunges @ workout load
- 3 x KB snatches/arm @ workout load
- 2 x Burpees over the bar

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2

**A) For Time:**
- 50 Thrusters (40/25 kg)
- 50 Burpees Over the Bar

Time cap: 5 min (expected: 3:30-4:30)

— 2:00 Rest —

**B) For Time:**
- 50 Front Rack Lunges (40/25 kg)
- 50 KB Snatches (24/16 kg)

Time cap: 5 min (expected: 2:30-3:30)

— 2:00 Rest —

**C) For Time:**
- 50 Thrusters (40/25 kg)
- 50 Burpees Over the Bar
- 50 Front Rack Lunges (40/25 kg)
- 50 KB Snatches (24/16 kg)

Time cap: 12 min (expected: 8-11 min)

*Split all reps any way between partners.*

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Pigeon stretch: 45s/side
3. Forearm stretch: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "Parts A and B taught us how hard we could push; Part C just asked for consistency."
