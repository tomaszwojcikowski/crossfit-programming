# FRIDAY: Team AMRAP & Gymnastic Chipper

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Jump Ropes, Pull-up Rig, Kettlebells, Wall Balls
*   **Stimulus:** Long team chipper with varied movements. Pacing and communication are key.
*   **Standards:** Full extension on T2B. Lockout on KB snatches. Full depth on wall balls.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Volume → Load → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** As written (200 DU, 24/16kg KB, 10/6kg WB).
    *   **B:** 400 SU, 16/12kg KB, 6/4kg WB, Knee raises.
    *   **C:** 200 SU, 12/8kg KB, 4/3kg WB, V-ups.
*   **Top Cues:** "Breathe on the wall balls." "Keep moving on the burpees." "Communicate on the T2B."
*   **Bottleneck:** Rig space for T2B. Stagger starts or use different movements if needed.

## Warmup (RAMP)
*Stimulus: Prep for long team chipper. Prime the aerobic system and shoulders.*

*Equipment: Jump Rope, Kettlebell, Wall Ball*

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** 2:00 Jump rope (easy pace) + 10 Air squats + 10 Push-ups
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** 1:30 Jump rope (moderate pace) + 8 KB deadlifts + 8 Wall ball front squats
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** 1:00 Jump rope (hard pace) + 5 KB snatches + 5 Wall ball push press
*   *Rest:* 30-60s between rounds.

## Mobility (90s)
*Open hips and shoulders.*

*   **Couch Stretch:** 45s/side. Open up hips after jumping and squats.
*   **Thread the Needle:** 45s/side. Release upper back and shoulders.

---

## Movement Prep (4 min)
*Practice transitions and pacing.*

*   10 DU + 4 T2B + 4 KB Snatches + 4 Wall Ball shots + 4 Burpee ot KB

---

## Workout

**TEAM OF 2: IN 26' WINDOW**
*Long team chipper. Break up reps and communicate to keep the pace high.*

*   **Buy in:** 200 DU / 400 SU (Split as needed)

*   **Then, AMRAP in remaining time:**
    *   **4 ROUNDS (I go, You go exercise):**
        *   8 T2B
        *   8 KB Snatches (24/16kg)
        *   8 Wall Ball shots (10/6kg)
        *   8 Burpee ot KB
    *   **Then:**
        *   48 T2B (Split as needed)
        *   48 KB Snatches (Split as needed)
        *   48 Wall Ball shots (Split as needed)
        *   48 Burpee ot KB (Split as needed)

*Time Cap: 26:00*

---
