# TUESDAY: Heavy Front Squats & Gymnastic AMRAP

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Barbell, Squat Rack, Box, Wall Space
*   **Stimulus:** Heavy front squat progression followed by a fast-paced triplet.
*   **Standards:** Full depth on squats. Lockout on HSPU. Full extension on box jumps.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** As written (HSPU, 60/50cm box, 45/30kg FS).
    *   **B:** Box HSPU or heavy DB press, 50/40cm box, 35/25kg FS.
    *   **C:** Push-ups, step-ups, 20/15kg FS.
*   **Top Cues:** "Elbows high." "Drive out of the hole." "Kick hard on the wall."
*   **Bottleneck:** Wall space for HSPU. Stagger starts or use DB press if needed.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)
*Stimulus: Prep for heavy front squats and overhead pressing.*

*Equipment: Empty Barbell, Box*

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** 2:00 Shuttle runs (easy pace) + 10 Air squats + 10 Push-ups
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** 1:30 Shuttle runs (moderate pace) + 8 Empty bar front squats + 8 Box step-ups
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** 1:00 Shuttle runs (hard pace) + 5 Empty bar thrusters + 5 Box jumps
*   *Rest:* 30-60s between rounds.

## Mobility (90s)
*Open hips and front rack.*

*   **Deep Squat Hold:** 45s. Hands clasped, elbows push knees out.
*   **Lat Stretch on Rig:** 45s/side. Arm overhead, lean away.

---

## Movement Prep (4 min)
*Build to starting weight for front squats.*

*   5 Front Squats (Empty Bar)
*   3 Front Squats (Light weight)
*   2 Front Squats (Working weight - 80%)

---

## Workout

**A) STRENGTH: Front Squats (Every 2' for 7 sets)**
*Build weight across the 7 sets based on 1RM.*

*   Set 1: 6 reps @ 80% 1RM
*   Set 2: 5 reps @ 85%
*   Set 3: 4 reps @ 87%
*   Set 4: 3 reps @ 90%
*   Set 5: 2 reps @ 93%
*   Set 6: 2 reps @ 93%
*   Set 7: 1 rep @ 96%

*Total Time: 14:00*

**[ Reset Window: 3-4:00 ]**
*   *Action:* Strip bars to metcon weight (45/30kg). Set up boxes and wall space.

**B) 12' AMRAP**
*Fast-paced triplet. Break up HSPU early to avoid failure.*

*   5 HSPU
*   10 Box Jumps (60/50cm)
*   15 Front Squats (45/30kg)

*Time Cap: 12:00*

---

## Cooldown (4 min)

**1. Downshift Breathing (60s)**
*   Easy walk. **Nasal breathing only.**

**2. Mobility (3 min)**
*   **Pigeon Pose:** 60s/side. Deep glute stretch after squats and jumps.
*   **Puppy Pose:** 60s. Open shoulders after HSPU.

---

**Debrief Prompts:**
*   "One bright spot?" (Pacing: "Kept moving on the box jumps.")
*   Stimulus check: "Did the front squats feel heavy but manageable?"
*   Takeaway: "Keep elbows up on the front squats."
