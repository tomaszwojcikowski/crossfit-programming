# THURSDAY: Push Press & Overhead Squat Intervals

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Barbell, Squat Rack, Running Route
*   **Stimulus:** Heavy push press progression followed by a fast-paced couplet.
*   **Standards:** Full lockout on push press. Full depth on OHS.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** As written (40/25kg OHS).
    *   **B:** 30/20kg OHS, 3 Lap run.
    *   **C:** 20/15kg OHS, 2 Lap run.
*   **Top Cues:** "Drive through the heels." "Lockout overhead." "Breathe on the run."
*   **Bottleneck:** Running route. Stagger starts or use machines if needed.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)
*Stimulus: Prep for heavy push press and overhead squats.*

*Equipment: Empty Barbell, Running Route*

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** 2:00 Run (easy pace) + 10 PVC pass-throughs + 10 Air squats
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** 1:30 Run (moderate pace) + 8 Empty bar strict press + 8 Empty bar front squats
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** 1:00 Run (hard pace) + 5 Empty bar push press + 5 Empty bar OHS
*   *Rest:* 30-60s between rounds.

## Mobility (90s)
*Open shoulders and hips.*

*   **Lat Stretch on Rig:** 45s/side. Arm overhead, lean away.
*   **Deep Squat Hold:** 45s. Hands clasped, elbows push knees out.

---

## Movement Prep (4 min)
*Build to starting weight for push press.*

*   5 Push Press (Empty Bar)
*   3 Push Press (Light weight)
*   2 Push Press (Working weight)

---

## Workout

**A) STRENGTH: Push Press (Every 1'15 for 6 sets)**
*Build weight across the 6 sets.*

*   Set 1-6: 5 reps

*Total Time: 7:30*

**[ Reset Window: 3-4:00 ]**
*   *Action:* Strip bars to metcon weight (40/25kg). Set up running route.

**B) Every 3' for 6 sets**
*Fast-paced couplet. Push the pace on the run to maximize rest.*

*   4 Lap run
*   15 OHS (40/25kg)

*Time Cap: 3:00 for each round*

---

## Cooldown (4 min)

**1. Downshift Breathing (60s)**
*   Easy walk. **Nasal breathing only.**

**2. Mobility (3 min)**
*   **Pigeon Pose:** 60s/side. Deep glute stretch after running and squats.
*   **Puppy Pose:** 60s. Open shoulders after push press and OHS.

---

**Debrief Prompts:**
*   "One bright spot?" (Pacing: "Held a consistent pace on the run.")
*   Stimulus check: "Did the push press feel heavy but manageable?"
*   Takeaway: "Keep elbows up on the OHS."
