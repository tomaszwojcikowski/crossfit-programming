# Wednesday (08/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Jump ropes, barbells, plates, pull-up rig
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Long team chipper inside a 25-minute AMRAP. Rope volume interrupts heavy barbell work and gymnastics, so handoffs matter.
- Scaling A: 80 double unders each set. Hang squat cleans at 40/25 kg. Toes-to-bar to hanging knee raises.
