# Wednesday (01/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Assault bike (AAB), rower, boxes (75/60 cm), barbells, plates
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team engine work — alternating 7' AMRAPs on machine + gymnastic/barbell pairings.
  Two full A→B cycles with 2' rests. Continuous output, clean handoffs.
- Scaling A: Reduce machine cals to 35/28. Box jumps → step-overs. Clean → hang power clean.
- Scaling B: Bike/row cals 25/20. Box step-overs only. Cleans 60/40 or DB hang cleans.
- Logistics: One team owns one bike lane, one row lane, one box, and one bar for the full class. Rehearse handoffs before the first AMRAP.
- Top Cues: "Share cals in chunks of 10." "Jump through the ground." "Elbows up on the catch."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy bike or row
  - 8 x Hip circles
  - 8 x Air squats
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Moderate bike or row
  - 6 x Box step-overs
  - 8 x Good mornings (bodyweight)
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Hard bike or row
  - 5 x Box jumps
  - 3 x Jump-and-shrug reps
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Hip flexor stretch: 45s/side
- Ankle / deep squat hold: 45s (box jump and clean prep)

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 3 x Box jumps — land and absorb
- 3 x Empty-bar hang power cleans
- 3 x Power clean @ working weight
- 1 x Team handoff rehearsal on machine

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 2 Cycles

**Each cycle:**

**A) 7' AMRAP:**
- 50 cal AAB (split any way)
- 25 Box Jumps (75/60 cm, split any way)

— 2 min rest —

**B) 7' AMRAP:**
- 50 cal Row (split any way)
- 25 Cleans (80/55 kg, split any way)

— 2 min rest —

**Repeat once (total 2 full cycles — 34 min)**

**Expected per AMRAP (A):** 1–2 rounds (fit teams: ~2; average: 1+)
**Expected per AMRAP (B):** 1–2 rounds (fit teams: ~2; average: 1)

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 2 min easy spin or walk
2. Figure-4 stretch (glute/hip): 45s/side
3. Thread the needle (thoracic rotation): 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "We had a clear switch plan and stuck to it."
