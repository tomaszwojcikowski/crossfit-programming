# Friday (03/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Dumbbells (22.5/16 kg), pull-up rig
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team I-go-you-go chipper in a 28' window — two blocks of alternating full rounds.
  First block: devil press + pull-ups. Second block: burpee pull-ups + lunges. Last minutes
  on single-DB devil presses until time is called.
- Scaling A: Reduce devil press load (16/12 kg). Pull-ups in small kipping sets.
  Burpee pull-ups → burpee + jumping pull-up.
- Scaling B: Single-arm devil press with lighter DB. Ring rows instead of pull-ups.
  Down-up + jumping pull-up instead of burpee pull-up. Lunges unloaded.
- Logistics: One team per rig bay and dumbbell pair. Agree on the handoff signal before Part 1 so the class can start together.
- Top Cues: "Hinge hard on the burpee — push the floor away." "Pull elbows back, not down."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog / shuttle runs
  - 10 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Good mornings (bodyweight)
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle runs
  - 8 x Scap pull-ups on rig
  - 6 x Down-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Hard shuttle runs
  - 4 x Kipping pull-ups or jumping pull-ups
  - 4 x Fast alternating lunges
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Half-kneeling hip flexor stretch: 30s/side
- Lat stretch on rig: 30s

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 2 x Devil press (working weight)
- 3 x Pull-ups or 4 x jumping pull-ups
- 2 x Burpee pull-ups
- 6 x Alternating lunges with DB
- 1 x Partner round rehearsal — agree on handoff signal

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 28' Window
**Each partner completes the full round before switching.**

- **Part 1 — 12 total sets (6 each):**
  - 6 Alt. Devil Press (22.5/16 kg)
  - 12 Pull-Ups
- **Part 2 — 12 total sets (6 each):**
  - 6 Burpee Pull-Ups
  - 12 Alt. Lunges with DB (22.5/16 kg)
- **Into remaining time:** 1-DB Devil Press singles, split continuously
- **Target:** Part 1 ~45-60s per set (~9-12 min). Part 2 ~50-70s per set (~10-14 min).
  Leave ~2-6 min for the final devil press cash-out.

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Pigeon (deep glute): 30s/side
3. Lat stretch on rig: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "Which switch stayed sharpest when the pace climbed?"
