# Monday (30/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Racks, barbells, plates
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Back squat volume build, then CrossFit Games Open 2026 QuarterFinals —
  a palindrome chipper mixing shuttle runs, overhead squats, and lateral burpees.
- Scaling A: Keep squat percentages. OHS → front squat or pressing snatch balance if overhead is limiting.
- Scaling B: Reduce OHS load one tier or swap to DB overhead carry + DB thruster. Burpees → down-ups.
- Logistics: One bar and one shuttle lane per athlete. Stage cones before the squat block so the class can reset fast.
- Top Cues: "Drive the floor away." "Push the bar to the ceiling on OHS." "Stay low on the shuttle turn."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog / shuttle runs
  - 8 x Squat-to-stand
  - 8 x Ankle rolls
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Lateral line shuffles over a floor mark
  - 8 x Tempo air squats
  - 6 x PVC pass-throughs
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 7.5 m shuttle accelerations with sharp turns
  - 5 x Box jumps or squat jumps
  - 5 x Press in squat with PVC
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Hip flexor / couch stretch: 45s/side
- Lat / underarm stretch on rig: 45s (overhead squat prep)

## Strength: Back Squat (14 min)
**Every 2:00 for 7 sets:**

- 8 @ 70%
- 8 @ 70%
- 8 @ 70%
- 5 @ 75%
- 2 @ 80%
- 2 @ 80%
- 1 @ 85%

## Reset Window (4 min)
- Strip racks, set up barbell for OHS. Stage shuttle run cones (7.5 m).

## Movement Prep (2 min)
- 3 x Empty-bar overhead squats
- 3 x Lateral burpee over bar
- 3 x OHS (working weight)
- 1 x Shuttle run turn practice (how to stay low and accelerate out)

## Main Conditioning: Open 2026 QuarterFinals (12 min TC)
**For Time — palindrome chipper:**

- 20 Shuttle Runs (1 = 7.5 m)
- 20 OHS (52/36 kg)
- 30 Lateral Burpees Over the Bar
- — 1 min rest —
- 30 Lateral Burpees Over the Bar
- 20 OHS (52/36 kg)
- 20 Shuttle Runs (1 = 7.5 m)

**Time cap:** 12 min (expected: 9–11 min fit / 11–12 min average)

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 2 min easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Pigeon (deep glute): 45s/side
3. Child's pose: 45s
4. Bright spot: "I held the overhead position through fatigue."
