# Tuesday (31/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Dumbbells or kettlebells (2x24/16 kg), sandbag/bumper plate (50/30 kg) for
  bearhug carry, wall balls, jump ropes, machine (rower/bike/ski)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Grip-to-core chipper — heavy carries bookend wall balls and double unders,
  finishing on a cal push. Expect legs and grip to fatigue early.
- Scaling A: Reduce carry loads by one tier. Single unders (2x count) instead of DU.
- Scaling B: Farmer carry to 200m with lighter implement; sandbag → light plate bearhug.
  Reduce wall ball target or load.
- Top Cues: "Tall spine on the carry." "Find the rhythm — let the ball do the work."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog / shuttle runs
  - 10 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Calf raises
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle runs
  - 8 x Goblet squats (light)
  - 10 x Single unders / 10 x DU attempts
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Hard shuttle runs
  - 5 x Wall balls (light)
  - 5 x Fast DU
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Hip flexor stretch: 45s/side (squat and carry opener)
- Wrist circles + forearm stretch: 45s (grip prep for carries)

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 20 m Farmer carry (working weight) — feel the load
- 20 m Bearhug carry (working weight) — set the spine
- 5 x Wall balls — find the catch height
- 10 x Double unders

## Main Conditioning: 2 Rounds for Time

**2 rounds:**
- 200 m Farmer Carry (2x24/16 kg)
- 50 Wall Ball Shots
- 200 m Bearhug Farmer Carry (50/30 kg)
- 50 Double Unders
- 50 cal Machine

**Time cap:** 24 min (expected: 18–21 min fit / 21–23 min average)

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 2 min easy walk or bike, nasal breathing
2. Seated hamstring fold: 45s
3. Supine twist (lumbar reset): 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "I kept moving on the carries — no big sets on the ground."
