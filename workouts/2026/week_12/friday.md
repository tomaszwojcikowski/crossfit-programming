# Friday (20/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbells, plates, kettlebells, open lane for bar-facing jumps
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team effort with short opener sets, then one longer grind where teams can split volume however they want.
- Scaling A: Keep deadlift loading, reduce bar-facing jump volume only if needed.
- Scaling B: Deadlift lighter, jumping over the bar -> line hops, kettlebell swings lighter.
- Top Cues: "Move clean, then move fast." "Split before grip blows up."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog / shuttle runs
  - 10 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Dead bugs / side
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle runs
  - 8 x Bootstrappers
  - 6 x Down-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Hard shuttle runs
  - 10 x Fast line hops
  - 6 x Down-ups
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Pike fold: 45s
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 4 x Deadlifts
- 4 x KB swings
- 4 x Jumping over the bar or fast line hops
- 3 x Down-ups
- 1 x Quick team handoff rehearsal

- 10 Deadlifts (80/50)
