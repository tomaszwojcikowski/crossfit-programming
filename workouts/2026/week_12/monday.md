# Monday (16/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Racks, barbells, plates, dumbbells, pull-up rig
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Squat volume under control, then Open 26.2-style ascending gymnastics test under fatigue.
- Scaling A: Keep back squat percentages. Pull-ups -> banded strict or jumping pull-ups. Muscle-ups -> chest-to-bar or high pull transitions.
- Scaling B: Lighter DBs. Reduce each gymnastics set to 15 reps to preserve the time domain.
- Top Cues: "Drive through the floor." "Break the pull before failure."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog / shuttle runs
  - 8 x Scap pulls on rig
  - 8 x Squat-to-stand
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle runs
  - 6 x Inchworms + shoulder taps
  - 8 x Walking lunges + overhead reach / side
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Hard shuttle runs
  - 5 x Kip swings
  - 5 x Squat jumps
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front rack / lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Deep squat hold: 45s

## Strength: Back Squat (14 min)
**Every 2:00 for 7 sets:**

- 8 @ 65%
- 8 @ 65%
- 8 @ 65%
- 5 @ 70%
- 2 @ 75%
- 2 @ 75%
- 1 @ 80%

## Reset Window (4 min)
- Strip racks, set dumbbells, and confirm pull-up / chest-to-bar / muscle-up scaling before the metcon.

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 1 x 6 m DB OH walking lunge
- 4 x Alternating DB snatches / arm
- 3 x Pull-ups or 3 x chest-to-bar singles
- 1 x Transition rehearsal for the athlete's top gymnastics option
