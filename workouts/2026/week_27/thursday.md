# Thursday (02/07/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Med ball (14/10 kg), rope, dumbbells (10/7.5 kg), wall space
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Five-station EMOM mixing cyclical power, pulling, isolation, and max-effort pressing. Athletes should hit each minute with intent and use the rest minute to reset.
- Scaling A: Med ball to 12/8 kg. Rope climbs to 1 rope climb or 3 rope pulls from seated. HSPU to 6 box-pike HSPU or 8 dumbbell push presses.
- Scaling B: Med ball to ground-to-shoulder. Rope climbs to rope pulls or strict pull-ups. HSPU to elevated push-ups or seated dumbbell press.
- Logistics: Pre-stage five stations in order around the room. Athletes rotate through the same sequence every 5 minutes.
- Top Cues: "Snap the hips on the med-ball throw." "Feet before hands on the rope." "Lock the elbows on the curl, then press tall on the HSPU."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, row, or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Bootstrappers
  - 8 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build pull and press rhythm
  - 6 x Med-ball squats
  - 4 x Rope pulls or scap pull-ups
  - 6 x Dumbbell curls light
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast med-ball reps
  - 2 x Rope climbs or scale
  - 3 x HSPU or Z-press scale
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 6 x Med-ball over shoulder @ workout load
- 1 x Rope climb or scale
- 10 x Dumbbell biceps curls @ workout load
- Max 20s HSPU or Z-press @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: 25' EMOM
Repeat this 5-minute cycle for 5 rounds:

1. **Min 1:** 8 Med-Ball Over the Shoulder
2. **Min 2:** 2 Rope Climbs
3. **Min 3:** 20 Biceps Curls (dumbbells)
4. **Min 4:** Max Handstand Push-Ups / Z-Press
5. **Min 5:** Rest

**Target:** finish prescribed work in 30-45s on work minutes; use rest minute to breathe and reset grip.

## Cooldown (3 min)

2. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
3. Supine twist: 45s/side
