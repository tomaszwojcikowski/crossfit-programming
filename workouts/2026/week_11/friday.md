# Friday (13/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbells (60/35 kg), Rig (BMU/Pull-ups)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Partner relay. Sprint pace intervals.
- Scaling A: Weight reduced for C&J. Jumping pull-ups instead of Pull-ups/BMU.
- Scaling B: Hang clean and press lightweight. Ring rows.
- Top Cues: "Drive hard to finish." "Keep moving right away."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Calorie Bike
  - 10 x Scap pull-ups
  - 10 x Lunges
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Calorie Bike
  - 8 x Combo Push-ups (PU + shoulder tap x2, PU + knee tap x2, PU + toe tap x2) x4
  - 8 x Kip swings
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Calorie Bike (Sprint)
  - 5 x Burpees (or foot jumps)
  - 4 x strict pull-ups
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Thoracic extension with bar: 45s

## Movement Prep (6 min)
- Barbell touchpoints: Deadlift, Clean pull, Muscle clean, Front squat, Jerk.
- Gymnastics: Scap pulls, kip swings, strict pull-up, BMU transition practice.

## Main Conditioning: Friday Friday! (25 min)
**Team of 2 - 20 Rounds for Time (I go, You go full round)**
*(Each partner does 10 rounds total)*

- 1 Cluster (60/35 kg)
- 3 Clean & Jerk (60/35 kg)
- 5 Burpee over the bar
- 7 Pull-ups (OR 3 Bar Muscle-Ups)

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. Slow walking (nasal down-regulation)
2. Pec/shoulder stretch against wall: 60s/side
3. Forearm wall stretch: 45s/side
4. Figure-4 glute stretch: 45s/side
5. Bright spot: "Stayed unbroken on C&J." Or "Partner transitions were perfect."
