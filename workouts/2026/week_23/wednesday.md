# Wednesday (03/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Machine, rope climb station, wall space, open run lane, barbell, plates
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Long team AMRAP with frequent handoffs. The machine taxes the lungs, the rope climb and wall walks punish sloppy changes, the run forces the team back together, and the hang cleans reward smooth rep sharing.
- Scaling A: Machine target to 100/84 calories. Rope climb to 2 legless pulls from the floor or 6-8 ring rows plus 2 wall walks. Hang cleans at 40/25 kg. Shorten the lap distance if needed.
- Scaling B: Machine target to 80/70 calories. Rope climb to 1 rope stand-up or 8 ring rows. Wall walks to 2 reps or inchworm-to-box. Hang cleans at 35/20 kg.
- Logistics: One team owns one machine, one rope lane, one wall lane, one bar, and one run route. Brief the change standard before class: every time the team switches off the machine at the prescribed calorie mark, complete the rope climb and wall walks before the next athlete starts.
- Top Cues: "Change on the number, not after it." "Stay calm on the rope." "Touch-and-go only if the bar path stays clean."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy machine work, jog, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Bootstrappers
  - 20s Dead hang
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Moderate machine build
  - 4 x Empty-bar hang cleans
  - 2 x Inchworms to plank
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s machine surge
  - 1 x Rope climb drill or rope lock practice
  - 1 x Wall walk or scale
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (6 min)
- 20/17 calorie relay rehearsal on the machine
- 1 rope climb drill and 1 wall walk each
- 5 hang cleans at workout load

## Main Conditioning: Team of 3 - 28' AMRAP
- 120/102 Calories on machine
  - Change every 20/17 calories
  - After each change: 1 Rope Climb + 3 Wall Walks
- 2 Laps run together
- 120 Hang Cleans (50/30 kg)

Split the hang cleans any way. One athlete works at a time on the machine unless your gym's house standard says otherwise.

**Expected rounds:** 0.7-1.2 rounds (most teams finish the machine and run, then chip well into the hang cleans; fitter teams may complete 1 full round)

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
