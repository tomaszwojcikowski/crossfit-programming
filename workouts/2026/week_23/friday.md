# Friday (05/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates, box, open bar lane
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Partner sprint-grind. Athletes alternate full rounds so each effort can stay aggressive, but the total 40 rounds demand controlled breathing and quick equipment handoffs.
- Scaling A: Thrusters and squat snatches at 30/20 kg. Burpee over bar to lateral burpees. Box jumps to box step-ups or lower height.
- Scaling B: Reduce each section to 16 rounds total if needed. Squat snatches to power snatch plus overhead squat or power snatch only if mobility is the limiter.
- Logistics: One bar and one box per team. Make the "I go, you go, full round" standard explicit before the clock starts so teams do not break reps inside a round.
- Top Cues: "Finish your round, then clear the lane." "Drive with the legs first." "Land soft and step down if needed."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jump rope, jog, bike, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Air squats
  - 4 x Burpees
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build foot speed and turnover
  - 5 x Empty-bar front squats
  - 5 x Empty-bar muscle snatches
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast feet or short shuttle burst
  - 3 x Empty-bar thrusters
  - 2 x Empty-bar overhead squats
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Ankle stretch on wall or box: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- Partner A practice round: 3 thrusters + 3 burpee over bar
- Partner B practice round: 2 squat snatches + 3 box jumps
- Build the bar to workout load

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 - For Time

20 Rounds total, alternating full rounds:
- 5 Thrusters (40/25 kg)
- 5 Burpee Over Bar

Then,

20 Rounds total, alternating full rounds:
- 3 Squat Snatches (40/25 kg)
- 5 Box Jumps (60/50 cm)

"I go, you go" means one athlete completes the full round before the partner starts.

**Time cap:** 27 min (expected: 18-27 min)

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Calf stretch on box or wall: 45s/side
