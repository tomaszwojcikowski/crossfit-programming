# Tuesday (02/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Bench or floor space, pull-up rig, wall space, machine
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: High-density upper-body EMOM where athletes should stay shy of failure across pressing, pulling, and inverted pressing while the machine station keeps the aerobic demand honest.
- Scaling A: Bench or floor press stays at 10-12 reps. Pull-ups to banded pull-ups or ring rows. Handstand push-ups to 8-12 box pike push-ups. Chin-ups to a supinated ring row.
- Scaling B: Use dumbbell floor press for 8-10 reps. Reduce each gymnastics station to 6-8 quality reps. Machine minute becomes 35s easy-hard instead of a full 45s push.
- Logistics: Set each athlete with one pressing station, one rig lane, one wall lane, and a clear machine order before the clock starts. Rep choices should be fixed before round 1.
- Top Cues: "Pick repeatable numbers." "Break before you fail." "Use the machine to breathe, not to redline."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy machine work or jump rope
  - 8 x Scap push-ups
  - 20s Active hang
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Moderate machine cadence
  - 8 x Empty-bar or PVC strict press
  - 4 x Kip swings or strict pull-up prep
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s machine surge
  - 4 x Light floor presses
  - 2 x Handstand kick-up or 4 pike shoulder taps
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Pec stretch on rig or wall: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 6 bench or floor press reps at working load
- 4 pull-ups or scale
- 3 handstand push-ups or scale
- 4 chin-ups or scale

## Main Conditioning: 25' EMOM
1. 10-15 Bench Press or Floor Press
2. 10-15 Pull-Ups or 6 Bar Muscle-Ups
3. 10-15 Handstand Push-Ups
4. 10-15 Chin-Ups
5. 45s Machine

Choose numbers you can hold for all 5 rounds with clean movement.

**Target:** finish stations 1-4 with 10-20s to reset. The machine minute should feel like a controlled push, not an all-out sprint.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk or very easy machine work
2. Doorway pec stretch: 45s/side
3. Child's pose with hands elevated: 45s
