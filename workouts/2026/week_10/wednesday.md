# WEDNESDAY: 30' EMOM Skills + Engine

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 hard cap | **Equip:** Rope, pull-up rig, jump rope, medball, wall target (120cm)
*   **Flow:** Warmup 10:00 → Mobility 1:30 → Skill Prep 8:00 → Reset 2:30 → EMOM 30:00 → Cooldown/Debrief 8:00
*   **Stimulus:** Cyclical mixed-skill density with one built-in rest station each 6-minute cycle.
*   **Standards:** HSW measured in 5m segments. Rope climb to target height. T2B both feet touch bar. Burpee-medball over wall = chest to floor + medball clears wall line.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Movement complexity → Volume → Load
*   **Scaling:**
	*   **A:** As written.
	*   **B:** 2 x 3-4m wall walk travel, 1 rope climb (or 2 rope pulls), 8 knee raises, 30-40 DU attempts.
	*   **C:** Bear crawl 10m, 6 burpee-over-line, 6 ring rows + 6 hanging knee raises, 40 fast singles.
*   **Top Cues:** "Kick to the line." "Pull long, lock feet." "Snap hips to the bar."
*   **Bottleneck Fix:** If rope stations are limited, use alternating rope minute starts by lane.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)
*Stimulus: Prime shoulders, grip, trunk tension, and bounce rhythm.*

*Equipment: Jump rope + rig*

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** 2:00 singles easy + 8 scap pulls + 8 hollow rocks
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** 1:30 rope moderate + 8 kip swings + 6 inchworms
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** 1:00 rope hard + 4 wall walk steps (1 full, 1 half) + 8 burpees
*   *Rest:* 30-60s between rounds.

## Mobility (90s)
*Brief overhead and front-body opening.*

*   **Puppy Pose:** 45s (lats/shoulders)
*   **Hanging Lat Stretch:** 45s/side (grip + overhead line)

---

## Skill Prep (8:00)
*Build movement quality before density work.*

*   2 x 5m HSW line drills (or wall walk travel)
*   1 rope climb + controlled descent (or 2 rope pulls)
*   6 T2B singles (or 8 knee raises)
*   20 DU smooth attempts (or 40 singles)

**[ Reset Window: 2:30 ]**
*Action: Assign all athletes to EMOM stations and confirm station order.*

---

## Main Conditioning

**A) 30' EMOM (5 rounds of 6 stations)**

1. 2 x 5m HSW
2. 6 Burpee-Medball over the wall (120cm)
3. 2 Rope Climbs
4. 12 T2B
5. Max DU / practice DU
6. Rest

*Score: completed work each station + DU quality/volume on minute 5.*

---

## Cooldown + Debrief (8:00)

**1) Downshift (2:00)**
*   Easy walk or spin, nasal breathing.

**2) Mobility (3:00)**
*   Thread the Needle (T-spine): 45s/side
*   Forearm Stretch on Floor: 30s/side
*   Child's Pose: 60s

**3) Debrief (3:00)**
*   One bright spot.
*   Stimulus check: where did quality drop first (rope, T2B, or DU)?
*   One takeaway: "Keep reps crisp, not rushed."
