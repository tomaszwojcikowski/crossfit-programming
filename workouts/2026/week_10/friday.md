# FRIDAY: Team 25' AMRAP Barbell Ladder

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 hard cap | **Equip:** Barbell + plates (40/25), open floor lane for burpee OTB
*   **Flow:** Warmup 10:00 → Mobility 1:30 → Barbell Prep 6:00 → Reset 2:30 → AMRAP 25:00 → Cooldown/Debrief 15:00
*   **Stimulus:** Long team effort with cyclical barbell volume and repeated synchro burpee interruptions.
*   **Standards:** Power clean to shoulders with control. Snatch to overhead lockout. Push press dip-drive-lockout. Synchro burpees = both athletes chest down/up together.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
	*   **A:** As written (40/25).
	*   **B:** 35/20 and optional split 20/30/40 reps.
	*   **C:** 30/15 with movement swap to hang power clean + hang power snatch + push press, burpees over line (not bar).
*   **Top Cues:** "Move the bar close." "Punch the lockout." "Match the burpee rhythm."
*   **Bottleneck Fix:** One bar per team staged before clock. Mark clear burpee lane per team.

## Warmup (RAMP)
*Stimulus: Elevate heart rate and prep hinge + overhead under fatigue.*

*Equipment: Floor space*

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** 2:00 easy shuttles + 8 air squats + 8 inchworms
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** 1:30 moderate shuttles + 10 hip hinges + 8 pike push-ups
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** 1:00 fast shuttles + 5 burpees + 6 broad jumps
