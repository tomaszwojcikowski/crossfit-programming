# THURSDAY: Upper Body EMOM + Team Sprint Rounds

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 hard cap | **Equip:** Pull-up rig, dumbbells, incline setup/bench option, wall for HSPU
*   **Flow:** Warmup 9:00 → Mobility 1:30 → EMOM 10:00 → Reset 4:00 → Prep 3:00 → Team FT 16:00-18:00 target → Cooldown/Debrief to 60:00
*   **Stimulus:** Pull/push accessory fatigue first, then fast partner rounds under accumulating shoulder fatigue.
*   **Standards:** Chin over bar on strict chin-ups. HSPU to standard depth/target. Full lockout on push-ups.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Complexity → Volume → Load
*   **Scaling:**
	*   **A:** As written.
	*   **B:** EMOM 4-6 banded strict chin-ups + 8-12 light curls; team piece 5 pike push-up + 5 chin-up + 10 push-up.
	*   **C:** EMOM ring rows + seated DB curls; team piece 8 box pike push-up + 8 ring rows + 10 incline push-up.
*   **Top Cues:** "Pull chest to bar." "Press the floor away." "Switch fast, stay clean."
*   **Bottleneck Fix:** If HSPU wall lanes are short, stagger team start by one round.
