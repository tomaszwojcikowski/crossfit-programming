# TUESDAY: Deadlift Intervals + Team AMRAP

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 hard cap | **Equip:** Barbell + plates, boxes (60/50), wall space
*   **Flow:** Warmup 9:00 → Mobility 1:30 → Strength 9:00 → Reset 4:00 → Prep 3:00 → AMRAP 15:00 → Cooldown/Debrief 8:30
*   **Stimulus:** Heavy hinge under short rest, then repeatable team rounds with gymnastics + posterior chain.
*   **Standards:** Deadlift starts from dead stop. Wall walk chest-to-wall control. Two-foot takeoff/landing over box.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
	*   **A:** As written.
	*   **B:** Strength at 100/60, WOD deadlift 70/45, step-over allowed.
	*   **C:** Strength 3 Romanian deadlift (moderate), WOD = 2 wall walk to target + 6 deadlift 50/35 + 8 low box step-overs.
*   **Top Cues:** "Push the floor away." "Walk feet to your hands." "Land soft, stay low."
*   **Bottleneck Fix:** If wall space is limited, rotate pairs by lane and start half teams at deadlift.
