# Thursday (26/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, dumbbells (22.5/15 kg), wall space for HSPU
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Butterfly pull-up skill block, then a punchy 5-round chipper testing pulling endurance,
  pressing, and DB power.
- Scaling A: Regular kipping pull-ups. Pike push-ups or DB press instead of HSPU.
- Scaling B: Banded strict pull-ups and chin-ups. DB press seated or standing.
- Top Cues: "Let the hips load the arch." "Press the floor away on HSPU."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog / shuttle runs
  - 8 x Scap pulls on rig
  - 8 x Dead bugs / side
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Shuttle runs
  - 6 x Kip swings
  - 8 x Down-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Hard shuttle runs
  - 5 x Kipping pull-ups or jumping pull-ups
  - 5 x Push-ups with push-through
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist circles + forearm stretch: 45s

## Skill: Butterfly Pull-Ups (15 min)
**Practice and drill — not for score:**

- Coach-led: kip timing, hip-to-bar connection, hand turnover
- Athletes work at their current tier:
  - A: Butterfly pull-ups — sets of 3-5 drilling rhythm
  - B: Kipping pull-ups — work on efficiency
  - C: Jumping pull-ups or ring rows — build lat activation
- Rest as needed; focus on quality over quantity

## Movement Prep (2 min)
- 3 x Double DB hang clean & jerk
- 3 x Strict chin-ups
- 1 x HSPU or 3 x pike push-ups

## Main Conditioning: 5 Rounds for Time

**5 rounds:**
- 10 Double DB Hang Clean & Jerk (22.5/15 kg)
- 10 Pull-Ups
- 10 HSPU
- 10 Chin-Ups
