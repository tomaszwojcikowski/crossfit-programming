# Wednesday (25/03/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rower / bike / ski erg, barbell, plates
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team grind — alternating machine calories with barbell cycling (OHS and snatch).
  Partners share all work however they choose within each station.
- Scaling A: Keep machine calories, reduce barbell load. OHS → front squat if overhead is limiting.
- Scaling B: DB snatches or muscle snatches instead of barbell. Reduce cals to 30/24.
- Top Cues: "Short sets with fast transitions." "Reach long overhead — push the bar to the ceiling."
