# Thursday (09/07/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, barbell or bench, plates (bench press or floor press load by athlete), open floor space
- Timing: 60 min hard cap (~50 min planned: warmup 10, mobility 1.5, prep 5, EMOM 30, cooldown 3)
- Stimulus: A 30-minute EMOM cycling six gymnastics and strength stations. Athletes should finish each work minute in 35-50s and use the remaining seconds to transition and reset. Volume is accumulated across 5 rounds of each station.
- Scaling A: Pull-ups to banded or jumping pull-ups. Pistols to single-leg box step-downs or assisted pistols. T2B to knees-to-elbows or high knee raises. HSPU to box-pike HSPU or 8 dumbbell push presses. Bench/floor press load to moderate 12-rep weight.
- Scaling B: Pull-ups to ring rows. Pistols to air squats (20 alternating). T2B to hanging knee raises. HSPU to seated dumbbell press (10 reps). Bench/floor press to light dumbbell floor press.
- Logistics: Athletes set up their bench or floor press position before class starts. Pull-up rig and open floor must handle all athletes cycling simultaneously. Announce station order and movement standards for pistols before starting.
- Top Cues: "Kip the pull-up early—don't wait until you're tired." "Press the heel into the floor on the pistol." "Lock the elbows out fully on every bench rep."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, row, or shuttle
  - 8 x Air squats
  - 6 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build pulling and pressing rhythm
  - 5 x Scap pull-ups
  - 5 x Ring rows or pull-up negatives
  - 6 x Glute bridges single leg
  - 6 x Push-ups
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast turnover
  - 4 x Pull-ups or scale
  - 4 x V-ups
  - 3 x HSPU or scale
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 8 x Pull-ups or scale
- 10 x Alternating pistols or scale (slow and controlled)
- 8 x T2B or scale
- 6 x HSPU or scale
- 10 x V-ups
- 8 x Bench or floor press @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: 30' EMOM

6-station cycle × 5 rounds. Each minute, complete the prescribed work:

1. **Min 1:** 15 Pull-Ups
2. **Min 2:** 10 Alternating Pistols (5/side)
3. **Min 3:** 15 Toes-to-Bar
4. **Min 4:** 12 Handstand Push-Ups
5. **Min 5:** 15 V-Ups
6. **Min 6:** 12 Bench Press / Floor Press

**Target:** finish each minute's work in 35-50s; stay consistent across all 5 rounds—don't go out too fast on round 1.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
3. Supine twist: 45s/side
