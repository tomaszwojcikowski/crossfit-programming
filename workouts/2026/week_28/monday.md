# Monday (06/07/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rack, barbell, plates (60/40 kg)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap (~55 min planned: warmup 10, mobility 1.5, squat ramp 5, strength 15, reset 3, prep 3, conditioning 15, cooldown 3)
- Stimulus: Heavy barbell squats at a percentage load, then fast barbell cycling and bodyweight under a 12-minute cap. Athletes should attack the 21s and keep moving on down-ups.
- Scaling A: Back squat to 70% 1RM or a challenging 5-rep load. Squat cleans to 45/30 kg or technique load; down-ups unbroken.
- Scaling B: Back squat to a 5-rep skill load (prioritise depth and brace). Squat cleans to front squats + hang power cleans or light load. Down-ups to 15-10-5.
- Logistics: One rack per athlete for the strength block. Strip to the conditioning load before the reset window. Mark the down-up line.
- Top Cues: "Brace before you un-rack." "Catch the clean in a full squat—don't catch high and squat after." "Chest and hips hit the floor together on the down-up."
