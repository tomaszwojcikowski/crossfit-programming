# Wednesday (08/07/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Plyo box (adjustable or stacked mats for box jump height test), barbell, plates (90/60 kg down to 40/25 kg)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap (~52 min planned: warmup 10, mobility 1.25, box jump test 8, reset 3, prep 3, for time 15, cooldown 3)
- Stimulus: A plyometric max-height test followed by a descending-weight front-rack lunge chipper broken up by burpees over the bar. The lunges get heavier in reps as the load drops; maintain quality footwork throughout.
- Scaling A: Box jump test to a safe challenging height (no reckless attempts). Front rack lunges: 70/45–60/40–50/35–35/22.5 kg. Burpees OTB to standard burpees facing the bar.
- Scaling B: Box jump test replaced with max box jump at comfortable height (4-6 attempts). Front rack lunges to 60/40–50/32.5–40/25–30/20 kg or dumbbell front-rack lunges. Burpees OTB to step-over burpees.
- Logistics: Set up a box jump station before class. Load four bars (or pre-load one bar and strip/add plates) to the four lunge loads. Mark the burpee line next to the bar.
- Top Cues: "Swing the arms and explode the hips—let the box come to you." "Front rack stays proud on every lunge." "Step off the box tall and move to the bar immediately."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog or bike
  - 8 x Air squats
  - 6 x Forward lunges alternating
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build hip and ankle prep
  - 5 x Step-ups each leg
  - 5 x Empty-bar front squats
  - 4 x Broad jumps
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x max-effort box jumps at a low-moderate height
  - 3 x Front-rack lunges light
  - 4 x Burpees over a PVC on the floor
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (75s)
- Hip flexor lunge stretch: 30-45s/side
- Ankle rocks: 30s/side

## Skill: 1RM Box Jump Height Test (8 min)

Find the highest box (or stack) you can jump to with two feet landing safely.

- Start at a comfortable height and add 5 cm increments.
- Allow 3-4 attempts near max.
- Stick the landing—hips through, no collapsing knees.

**Time cap: 8 min**

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Load barbell for the first lunge set. Mark the OTB line.

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 6 x Front rack lunges @ opening weight (90/60 kg)
- 4 x Burpees over the bar @ smooth pace

## Main Conditioning: For Time

- 6 Front Rack Lunges (90/60 kg)
- 6 Burpees Over the Bar
- 12 Front Rack Lunges (70/45 kg)
- 6 Burpees Over the Bar
- 18 Front Rack Lunges (50/35 kg)
- 6 Burpees Over the Bar
- 24 Front Rack Lunges (40/25 kg)
- 6 Burpees Over the Bar

**Time cap: 15 min (expected: 11-14 min)**

**Target:** complete each lunge set unbroken or in 2 sets max; move steadily on burpees—no sprinting, no stopping.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Couch stretch: 45s/side
3. Calf stretch on wall: 45s/side
