# Friday (26/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, kettlebell (24/16 kg), open floor
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Partner gymnastics and kettlebell window. The fixed early rounds should be smooth enough to buy time for a hard burpee-pull-up cash-out.
- Scaling A: 5 BMU to 8 chest-to-bar pull-ups or 10 pull-ups. Kettlebell at 20/12 kg. Burpee pull-ups to burpees plus jumping pull-ups.
- Scaling B: Pulling to ring rows or jumping pull-ups. Kettlebell at 16/12 kg. Snatches to Russian swings if overhead is limited. Hang cleans to dead-clean singles or goblet cleans.
- Logistics: One team per rig lane and kettlebell. Review "I go, you go, full round" before the clock starts so partners do not split reps inside a round.
- Top Cues: "Own the pull before you turnover." "Keep the bell close on the snatch." "Hit the ground, pop up, and jump straight to the bar."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, row, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Scap pull-ups
  - 8 x Kettlebell deadlifts light
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build pull and kettlebell rhythm
  - 4 x Kip swings or ring rows
  - 5 x Kettlebell swings light
  - 4 x Alternating kettlebell snatches light
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s quick jump-and-grab or fast feet
  - 2 x BMU transitions or pull-up scale
  - 3 x Hang kettlebell cleans each side
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 3 x BMU or pull-up scale
- 5 x Kettlebell swings @ workout load
- 5 x Alternating kettlebell snatches @ workout load
- 5 x Hang kettlebell cleans @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 25' Window
**Partners alternate full rounds for the first two blocks.**

- **Part 1:** 12 total rounds
  - 5 Bar Muscle-Ups or 8 Pull-Ups
  - 10 Kettlebell Swings (24/16 kg)
- **Part 2:** 12 total rounds
  - 10 Kettlebell Snatches (24/16 kg)
  - 10 Hang Kettlebell Cleans (24/16 kg)
- **Into remaining time:**
  - Max Burpee Pull-Ups

**Expected flow:** Part 1 ~7-10 min, Part 2 ~8-11 min, leaving roughly 4-8 min for the burpee-pull-up cash-out.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
3. Child's pose with side reach: 45s/side
4. Bright spot: "Our handoffs were clean enough to earn real time on the burpee-pull-up finish."
