# Thursday (25/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Wall space, barbell, plates (90/60 kg), wall ball (10/6 kg)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Repeatable interval work alternating upper-body pressing plus hinge strength with cyclical wall-ball and trunk volume. Athletes should finish each window with control, not collapse into missed reps.
- Scaling A: HSPU to 8 box-pike HSPU or 10 dumbbell push presses. Deadlift to 80/50 kg. Wall ball to 9/6 kg.
- Scaling B: HSPU to elevated push-ups. Deadlift to 60/40 kg. Wall ball to air squats or light med-ball thrusters. V-ups to tuck-ups.
- Logistics: Split the room into wall stations and wall-ball lanes with barbells centered between them. The class alternates A and B together every 3 minutes.
- Top Cues: "Own the kip before you press." "Push the floor away on the deadlift." "Catch tall and breathe at the top of the wall ball."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, row, or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Inchworms
  - 8 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Press and hinge rhythm
  - 5 x Pike push-ups or box shoulder taps
  - 5 x Empty-bar deadlifts
  - 6 x Wall-ball squats
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast wall-ball cadence
  - 2 x HSPU or scale
  - 3 x Deadlifts @ light build load
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig or wall: 45s/side
- Hamstring fold: 45s

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 3 x HSPU or chosen scale
- 5 x Deadlifts @ workout load
- 8 x Wall-ball shots @ workout load
- 8 x V-ups

## Main Conditioning: Every 3:00 for 10 Sets
Alternate A and B for 5 rounds each:

- **A:** 10 Handstand Push-Ups + 10 Deadlifts (90/60 kg)
- **B:** 20 Wall Ball Shots (10/6 kg) + 20 V-Ups

**Target:** finish each station in 75-120s and use the remaining time to reset for the next 3-minute window.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Child's pose: 45s
3. Supine twist: 45s/side
