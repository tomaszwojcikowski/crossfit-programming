# Monday (22/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rack, barbell, plates, dumbbells (22.5/15 kg), wall space
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Front squat volume builds controlled leg fatigue before a shoulder-and-unilateral AMRAP that rewards steady movement and clean transitions.
- Scaling A: Front squat at 55-57% if 60% turns into grinding reps. Wall walks to 2 pike walks or 4 shoulder taps against the wall. Dumbbells at 20/12.5 kg.
- Scaling B: Front squat to 5 x 8. Wall walks to inchworm + box shoulder taps. Walking lunges bodyweight or 2 x 10 kg. Alternating snatches to Russian swings or single-dumbbell clean and push press.
- Logistics: One rack per athlete for squats. Pre-stage dumbbells and wall lanes before the strength block ends so the reset is short.
- Top Cues: "Drive up at the same speed you descend." "Keep ribs down on the wall walk." "Push through the floor on every lunge step."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, bike, row, or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Air squats
  - 6 x Inchworms
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build squat and shoulder rhythm
  - 5 x Empty-bar front squats
  - 6 x Alternating reverse lunges
  - 6 x Down-dog shoulder taps
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast feet or short shuttle burst
  - 1 x Wall walk to comfortable range
  - 4 x Alternating dumbbell snatches light
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Calf stretch on wall: 45s/side

## Strength: Front Squat (13 min)
**Every 2:30 for 5 sets:**

- 10 Front Squats @ 60% 1RM

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Strip bars, claim wall lanes, and move dumbbells to workout space.

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 1 x Wall walk or chosen scale
- 5 x Dumbbell walking lunges each side @ workout load
- 6 x Alternating dumbbell snatches @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: 16' AMRAP

- 2 Wall Walks
- 10 Dumbbell Walking Lunges (22.5/15 kg, total steps)
- 20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatches (22.5/15 kg)

**Expected rounds:** 4-6 with unbroken snatches and controlled wall-walk pacing.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Hip flexor stretch: 45s/side
3. Child's pose with reach: 45s/side
