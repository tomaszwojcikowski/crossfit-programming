# MONDAY: Front Squats & Complex

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Rower/Bike, Barbell, Rig, (Roller)
*   **Stimulus:** Heavy legs + crisp bar speed. No grinding.
*   **Standards:** Full depth. Elbows high. No soft knees.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** % work + complex as written (full stimulus)
    *   **B:** RPE 7-8, hang power clean, push jerk (reduce load, preserve movement)
    *   **C:** DB front squat/press OR clean pulls + squat (load-reduced, same pattern)
*   **Top Cues:** "Elbows to wall." "Drive bar up." "Drop and launch."
*   **Bottleneck:** Share racks (alt sets) or pre-load bars.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** Hinge to reach x10, Squat-to-stand x6
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** Push-up to Down Dog x8, Pause squat x8
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** Jump squat x6, Fast elbows x10
*   *Cues:* "Push floor away." "Spin bar fast."

## Mobility (60-90s)

*   Front rack (rig): 30s/side
*   Ankle (knee to wall): 30s/side
*   Puppy Pose: 30s

---

## Workout

**A) Strength: Front Squat (E2MOM x7)**
*   8@67%, 8@67%, 8@67%, 5@72%, 2@77%, 2@77%, 1@82%

**[ Reset Window: 5:00 ]**
*   *Action:* Racks down. Couch stretch (45s). Deep squat (30s).
*   *Test:* 2 jumps. If slow: drop load 5-10%.
*   *Goal:* Restore breathing. Prepare for power.

**Prep (Empty Bar):**
*   5 DL → 5 HPC → 5 FS → 5 Push Press → 3 Jerk → 2 Full

**B) Barbell Complex (E2MOM x7)**
*   5 Deadlift (Fast pull)
*   4 Hang Clean (Max speed)
*   3 Front Squat (Explosive)
*   2 Push Press (Violent drive)
*   1 Jerk (Fast lockout)

---

## Cooldown (5-7 min)

**Protocol: Neural Reset (Post-Heavy Lifting)**

*   **Spinal Decompression (60s)**
    *   Dead hang from pull-up bar (use straps if needed).

*   **Walk & Breathe (2 min)**
    *   Easy walk, nasal breathing. Box breathing pattern (4-4-4-4).

*   **Mobility (3-4 min):**
    *   **Reclined Twist:** 60s/side. Release lumbar tension.
    *   **Pigeon Pose:** 60s/side. Deep hip rotators after squats.

*   **Debrief (during mobility):**
    *   *Bright Spot:* "Bar speed crisp in the complex?"
    *   *Takeaway:* "Elbows to wall" prevents forward lean.
