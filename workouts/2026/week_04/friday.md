# FRIDAY: Devil Press & Machine

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Machine, DBs, Box, Ball
*   **Stimulus:** Aerobic grind + clean mechanics.
*   **Standards:** Chest down. DBs overhead. Controlled step.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** As written (full stimulus)
    *   **B:** Lighter DBs / lower box / reduced cals (45/35) (reduce load, preserve movement)
    *   **C:** Burpee + push press; step-ups (not step-overs) (simplify pattern)
    *   **Pregnancy:** Elevated push-up on box + step-ups; step-back pattern (no jump)
*   **Top Cues:** "Sit to wall." "Breathe low." "Smooth step."
*   **Bottleneck:** Pre-assign machine order. Share Wall Ball.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)

*   **R1 (Mach 2:00):** DB DL x8, Alt Lunge x12
*   **R2 (Mach 1:30):** Hang High Pull x6, Push-up Down Dog x8
*   **R3 (Mach 1:00):** Power Snatch x4, Thruster jump x8
*   *Cues:* "Step far." "Push floor away." "Launch hands."

## Mobility (60-90s)

*   Cossack squat, Band shoulder, Hip flexor

---

## Workout

**A) Team of 2: 24' AMRAP**
*   30 Devil Press (2x22.5/15)
*   30 Box Step Over (2x50)

**[ Reset Window: 3:00 ]**
*   *Action:* Stage machine + wall ball.
*   *Script:* "First 4 mins easy. Hold pace."

**Prep:**
*   Devil Press (4-6), Step-over (6), Wall Ball (5)

**B) For Time (Tc: 9')**
*   60/50 Cal Machine
*   60 Wall Ball Shots (10/6)

---

## Cooldown

*   **Flush (2-4 min):** Slow machine or casual walk.
*   **Mobility:**
    *   Puppy Pose (shoulder/chest): 60s. Sink the head below the arms; active reach.
    *   Couch (psoas/quad): 45s/side. Keep the tailbone tucked and chest up.
    *   Legs Up Wall (parasympathetic): 1-2 min. Absolute stillness; focus on the belly rising.
*   **Debrief:**
    *   *Breathing:* 1 min focused nasal breathing.
    *   *Bright Spot:* "Highlight a smooth transition in the team AMRAP."
    *   *Check:* Did the first 4 mins of the AMRAP feel 'easy'?
    *   *Takeaway:* "Smooth is fast" on box step-overs.
