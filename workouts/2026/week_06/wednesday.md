# WEDNESDAY: E3MOM Alternating Couplets

**Setup:**
*   **Time:** 60:00 Cap | **Equip:** Machine (Row/Bike/Ski), Barbell (50/25 kg), Box (60/50 cm), Pull-up Bar
*   **Stimulus:** Mixed modal intervals—engine + barbell vs explosive + gymnastics.
*   **Standards:** Full hip extension on STO. Both feet on box. Chin over bar.
*   **Scaling Priority:** Load → Volume → Complexity
*   **Scaling:**
    *   **A:** As written (30/24 cal, 50/25 kg STO, 60/50 box, pull-ups)
    *   **B:** 24/18 cal, 40/20 kg STO, 50/40 box step-overs, banded pull-ups
    *   **C:** 18/14 cal, 30/15 kg STO, box step-ups, ring rows
*   **Top Cues:** "Press through the ceiling." "Land soft on the box." "Pull the bar to your chest."
*   **Bottleneck:** Machine + barbell transitions. Stage barbells near machines.

---

## Warmup (RAMP)
*Stimulus: Machine + STO + box jumps + pull-ups. Prime shoulders, hips, and engine.*

*   **R1 (2:00 easy):** 2:00 easy machine + 8 Air squats + 8 Shoulder press (empty bar)
*   **R2 (1:30 mod):** 1:30 moderate machine + 8 Box step-ups + 6 Push press (light)
*   **R3 (1:00 hard):** 1:00 hard machine + 5 Box jumps (step down) + 5 Explosive pull-ups
*   *Cues:* "Press through the ceiling." "Land soft."

## Mobility (60-90s)
*Open shoulders for STO + hips for box jumps.*

*   **Front Rack Stretch:** 45s. Elbows on box, press chest down. (STO catch + cycling)
*   **Pigeon Pose:** 30s/side. Hips square, relax forward. (Box jump landing + recovery)

---

## Workout

**Prep (Movement-Specific Primer, 3-5 min):**
*   Machine: 30s moderate pace, check settings
*   Barbell:
    *   5 Strict press + 5 Push press + 3 Push jerk (build to working weight)
*   Box: 5 step-ups + 5 box jumps (working height)
*   Rig: 5 Scap pulls + 5 Pull-ups (or scale)
*   *Goal:* Find pacing for 3:00 windows.

**A) E3MOM x 4 Sets (24:00 total)**

**Minute 0, 6, 12, 18 (Couplet A):**
*   30/24 Cal Machine
*   20 Shoulder-to-Overhead (50/25 kg)

**Minute 3, 9, 15, 21 (Couplet B):**
*   30 Box Jumps Over (60/50 cm)
*   20 Pull-ups

*Score: Time to complete each couplet. Target: finish each within 2:30.*

---

## Cooldown (5-7 min)

**Protocol: Metabolic Flush (Post-Interval)**
*4 sets of high-output couplets = significant lactate + shoulder/hip fatigue.*

**1. Immediate Reset (30-45s)**
*   **Physiological Sighs:** 5-10 cycles. Double inhale → long exhale.
    *Stay upright. Breathing resets autonomic state after interval work.*

**2. Active Flush (4 min)**
*   Easy bike or row at 50-60% Max HR.
*   **Nasal breathing only** (activates parasympathetic, maintains muscle pump).
*   *Target: Clear lactate from STO cycling + box jump volume.*

**3. Static Stretching (3 min)**
*STO volume = anterior shoulder tightness. Box jumps = hip flexor/calf stress.*
*   **Doorway Pec Stretch:** 45s/side. Arm at 90°, lean through.
    *Opens pec major/minor after overhead pressing volume.*
*   **Couch Stretch:** 45s/side. Knee to wall, squeeze glute.
    *Rectus femoris—releases hip flexor tension from box jumps.*
*   **Calf Stretch:** 30s/side. Wall lean, back leg straight.
    *Gastrocnemius recovery after 120 box jumps.*

**4. Debrief (during stretching)**
*   *Downshift breathing:* Slow nasal exhales.
*   *Bright spot examples:* "Finished all sets within time?" "Smooth machine transitions?" "Pull-ups stayed consistent?"
*   *Tech takeaway:* "Press through the ceiling, lock it out."
