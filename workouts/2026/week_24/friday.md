# Friday (12/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates, machine, single dumbbell (22.5/15 kg), open run lane
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Partner sprint-grind. Three rounds pair a shared run, a heavy barbell complex, machine calories, and synchronized devil presses. Teams should move with intent but keep handoffs clean.
- Scaling A: Complex at 50/30 kg. Machine to 50/40 calories. Devil press to 15/10 kg dumbbell. Shorten the lap if needed.
- Scaling B: Complex to power clean plus front squats plus push jerk at 40/25 kg. Machine to 40/35 calories. Devil press to 8 synchronized reps.
- Logistics: One bar, one machine, and one dumbbell per team. The source board programs teams of 2 — brief the shared run, complex lane, and synchro devil-press standard before the clock starts.
- Top Cues: "Run together, then split the work." "Receive the clean before you squat." "Match your partner's pace on the devil press."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, bike, row, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Air squats
  - 4 x Burpees
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build foot speed and turnover
  - 5 x Empty-bar front squats
  - 5 x Empty-bar muscle snatches
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast feet or short shuttle burst
  - 1 x Power clean + 2 front squats + 3 jerks, empty bar
  - 2 x Alternating devil presses light
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Ankle stretch on wall or box: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 1 short lap together at workout pace
- 2 x Complex @ starting load (1 power clean + 2 front squats + 3 jerks)
- 10 calories easy on the machine
- 4 x Alternating devil presses @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 3 Rounds For Time

- 1 Lap run together
- 10 Complex: 1 Power Clean + 2 Front Squats + 3 Jerk (60/35 kg)
- 60/50 Calories on machine
- 10 Synchronized Alternating Devil Press, 1 Dumbbell (22.5/15 kg)

Split the complex and machine work however the team chooses unless your gym standard says otherwise. Devil presses must stay synchronized.

**Time cap:** 29 min (expected: 18-26 min)

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
