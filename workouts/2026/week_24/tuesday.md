# Tuesday (09/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, jump rope, dumbbells (22.5/15 kg), target for burpees
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Nine short 2-minute windows rotating gymnastics, pulling, and dumbbell-plus-rope turnover. Athletes should clear each buy-in with enough time left to breathe before the next station.
- Scaling A: Toes-to-bar to 10 hanging knee raises. Bar muscle-ups to 4 bar muscle-ups or 8 chest-to-bar pull-ups. Pull-ups to banded pull-ups. Burpee jump 180° to standard burpee. Double-unders to 60 single-unders.
- Scaling B: Knee raises to V-ups on the floor. Pull-ups to ring rows. Dumbbell snatches at 17.5/10 kg for 16 reps. Double-unders to penguin taps or line hops.
- Logistics: Set rig lanes, rope zones, dumbbells, and the burpee target before class. Athletes rotate A → B → C across the 9 sets so every lane is claimed before round 1.
- Top Cues: "Snap to hollow on the rig." "Land soft, turn fast." "Keep the dumbbell close on the turnover."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jump rope or jog
  - 8 x Scap pull-ups
  - 8 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Rope rhythm build or line hops
  - 6 x Kip swings
  - 4 x Alternating dumbbell snatches light
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast rope cadence
  - 2 x Bar muscle-up transition drills or chest-to-bar singles
  - 4 x Burpees to target
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 4 x Toes-to-bar or scale
- 2 x Bar muscle-ups or pull-up scale
- 4 x Alternating dumbbell snatches @ workout load
- 20 x Double-unders or 30 x single-unders

## Main Conditioning: Every 2:00 for 9 Sets
Rotate through A, B, and C:

- **A:** 15 Toes-to-Bar + 8 Burpees to Target
- **B:** 8 Bar Muscle-Ups or 16 Pull-Ups + 8 Burpee Jump 180°
- **C:** 20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatches (22.5/15 kg) + 40 Double Unders

**Target:** finish each set in ~75-90s and keep 30-45s to reset before the next station.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
3. Figure-4 glute stretch: 45s/side
