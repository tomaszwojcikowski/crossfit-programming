# Tuesday (16/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates, rack or platform, jump rope
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Progressive power clean and jerk singles build to near-max effort, then benchmark DT tests barbell cycling and rope turnover under a tight clock.
- Scaling A: Power clean and jerk percentages down 3-5% if catch positions break. DT at 50/30 kg. Double-unders to 60 single-unders after each round.
- Scaling B: Power clean plus push jerk if split jerk is unstable. DT to power clean, hang power clean, push jerk at 40/25 kg. Double-unders to penguin taps or 45 single-unders.
- Logistics: One bar per athlete for the EMOM blocks. Stage a second bar or confirm strip-and-reload flow before DT so the class does not hunt for plates after the rest window.
- Top Cues: "Stay over the bar on the pull." "Dip straight, drive straight." "Set the back before every deadlift."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy row, bike, or jog
  - 8 x PVC pass-throughs
  - 8 x Air squats
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** PVC rhythm work
  - 5 x PVC clean pulls
  - 5 x Empty-bar power cleans
  - 5 x Empty-bar push jerks
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast rope cadence
  - 2 x Power clean + push jerk, empty bar
  - 3 x Hang power cleans light
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front-rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Strength / Skill: Power Clean & Jerk Progression (16 min)

- **5' EMOM:** 1 Power Clean & Jerk @ 81-85%
- Rest 1 min
- **4' EMOM:** 1 Power Clean & Jerk @ 84-88%
- Rest 1 min
- **3' EMOM:** 1 Power Clean & Jerk @ 87-91%
- Rest 2 min

**Target:** finish each rep in ~20-35s with time to reset and add load between blocks.

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 3 x Empty-bar deadlifts
- 2 x Hang power cleans @ DT load
- 2 x Jerks @ DT load
- 20 x Double-unders or 30 x single-unders

## Main Conditioning: 4 Rounds "DT" + Double-Unders For Time

Each round:
- 12 Deadlifts (60/35 kg)
- 9 Hang Power Cleans (60/35 kg)
- 6 Jerks (60/35 kg)
- Then 30 Double-Unders

**Time cap:** 13 min (expected: 9-13 min)

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30-45s/side
3. Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
