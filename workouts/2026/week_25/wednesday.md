# Wednesday (17/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Bench or floor-press setup, dip station or boxes, box (75/60 cm), pull-up rig, open floor for handstand walk
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Thirty-minute EMOM cycles through six stations in order — press, dips, jumps, toes-to-bar, rest, handstand walk — five times. Athletes should treat each minute as its own task and use the rest minute to reset before HSW.
- Scaling A: Bench or floor press to a load that allows 10 crisp reps each minute. Box dips to 10 reps with feet assistance if needed. Toes-to-bar to 12 hanging knee raises. Handstand walk to 2 x 10 m or 30s wall-facing hold.
- Scaling B: Floor press with dumbbells. Box dips to bench dips. Box jumps to step-ups. Toes-to-bar to V-ups on the floor. Handstand walk to pike walks or box shoulder taps.
- Logistics: Stage stations in workout order before minute 1: bench → dips → box → rig → rest lane → handstand walk wall. Athletes stay in the same lane and advance station to station each minute.
- Top Cues: "Drive the floor away on the press." "Lock out at the top of the dip." "Snap to hollow on the rig."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, bike, row, or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Scap push-ups
  - 8 x Dead bugs
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build pressing rhythm
  - 6 x Push-ups
  - 4 x Box dips or bench dips
  - 4 x Kip swings
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s wall-facing shoulder taps or kick-up hold
  - 3 x Box jumps bodyweight
  - 3 x Toes-to-bar or knee raises
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Pec stretch on rig or wall: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 5 x Bench or floor press, building to workout load
- 5 x Box dips or scale
- 4 x Box jumps @ workout height
- 4 x Toes-to-bar or scale
- 10 m handstand walk practice or scale

## Main Conditioning: 30' EMOM
Repeat this 6-minute sequence for 5 rounds:

1. 10 Heavy Bench or Floor Press
2. 10-15 Box Dips
3. 10 Box Jumps (75/60 cm)
4. 20 Toes-to-Bar
5. Rest
6. 2 x 5 m Handstand Walk

**Target:** finish each work minute in ~40-50s. Use minute 5 of each round to reset grip and shoulders before handstand walk.

## Cooldown (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Shoulder stretch on rig: 45s/side
3. Wrist flexor stretch: 30s/side
