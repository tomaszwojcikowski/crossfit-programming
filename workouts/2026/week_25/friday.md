# Friday (19/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Wall-ball target, kettlebells (24/16 kg), open floor for push-ups
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Partner grind. Five rounds pair synchronized push-ups, burpee deadlift kettlebell swings, and high-volume wall balls. Teams should match pace early so round 5 still moves.
- Scaling A: Burpee deadlift KB to 20/14 kg. Wall ball to 9/6 kg. Reduce synchronized push-ups to 15 per round.
- Scaling B: Push-ups from knees or incline, still synchronized. Burpee deadlift KB to 16/12 kg. Wall ball to 6/4 kg. Reduce to 4 rounds if needed.
- Logistics: Source board programs teams of 2. One kettlebell and one wall ball per team. Brief the synchronized push-up and burpee-deadlift standard before the clock starts.
- Top Cues: "Match your partner on every push-up." "Hinge first, then snap on the swing." "Catch and absorb the wall ball."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, bike, row, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Air squats
  - 6 x Push-ups
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Build turnover
  - 4 x Burpees
  - 6 x Kettlebell deadlifts light
  - 6 x Wall ball shots light
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast feet or short shuttle burst
  - 3 x Burpee deadlift kettlebell swings light
  - 5 x Wall ball shots @ workout load
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s
- Shoulder stretch on rig: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 10 synchronized push-ups (partner standard)
- 5 x Burpee deadlift KB swings @ workout load
- 10 x Wall ball shots @ workout load

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 5 Rounds For Time

- 20 Synchronized Push-Ups
- 30 Burpee Deadlift Kettlebell Swings (24/16 kg)
- 40 Wall Ball Shots (10/6 kg)

Split the burpee deadlifts and wall balls however the team chooses. Push-ups must stay synchronized.

**Time cap:** 28 min (expected: 18-26 min)

2. Quad stretch on wall or box: 45s/side
