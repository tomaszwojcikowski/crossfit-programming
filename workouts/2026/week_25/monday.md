# Monday (15/06/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rack, barbell, plates, kettlebell (24/16 kg), pull-up rig
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Front squat volume at moderate load builds leg strength, then a short mixed-modal chipper tests squat pattern, pulling, and hip hinge turnover under fatigue.
- Scaling A: Front squat at 60-62% if 65% overshoots the day. Goblet squat to 20/14 kg. Pull-ups to banded or 8 reps. KB swings to Russian swings at same load.
- Scaling B: Reduce front squat to 5 x 7. Goblet squat to 16/12 kg. Pull-ups to ring rows. Down-ups to step-back burpees. KB swings to 16/12 kg.
