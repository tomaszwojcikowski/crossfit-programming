# Friday (31/07/2026)

**Setup**

- **Equipment**: pull-up rig (burpee pull-ups, T2B, HSPU), lap run lane
- **Timing**: 60-min class cap; team for-time 27-min cap
- **Stimulus**: long partner chipper with shared runs and large gymnastics volumes; buy-in and buy-out bookend the piece
- **Scaling A**: burpee pull-ups → burpee + jumping pull-up; T2B → hanging knee raises; HSPU → pike HSPU / box HSPU; keep partner split
- **Scaling B**: burpee + ring row; sit-ups or V-ups; hand-release push-ups or DB strict press; reduce total volume ~25% if needed
- **Logistics**: one rig lane per team; brief who splits which reps; runs are **together**
- **Top Cues**:
  - **Burpee pull-up**: “Jump, pull, reset fast.”
  - **T2B**: “Short sets, quick switch.”
  - **HSPU**: “Kick up once, lock out every rep.”

## Warmup (8-10 min)

- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy)**: 2:00 easy jog or bike
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate)**: 2 rounds:
  - 4 down-ups
  - 4 scap pulls + 4 kip swings
  - 6 hollow rocks
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard)**: 2 rounds:
  - 2 burpee pull-ups (or scale)
  - 3 T2B / hanging knee raises
  - 2 pike push-ups / HSPU practice

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)

- 30-45s lat stretch each side
- 30-45s wrist extension stretch

## Movement Prep

Partner rehearsal (keep tiny):

- 2 burpee pull-ups each
- 4 T2B (split)
- 2 HSPU or scale each
- 50-100m easy jog together (lap feel)

## Main Conditioning

### Team of 2 — For Time

**Buy-in:** 60 **Burpee Pull-Ups** (split as needed)

Then **2 rounds:**

- `1 Lap Run` **together**
- 60 **Toes-to-Bar** (split)

Then **2 rounds:**

- `1 Lap Run` **together**
- 40 **Handstand Push-Ups** (split)

**Buy-out:** 60 **Burpee Pull-Ups** (split as needed)

**Time cap: 27 min (expected: 18-25 min)**

**Scaling options**

- **Scaling A**: burpee + jumping pull-up, hanging knee raises, pike/box HSPU, keep run together
- **Scaling B**: burpee + ring row, sit-ups/V-ups, HR push-ups, reduce buy-in/out to 40 if needed

## Cooldown & Debrief

- 2:00 easy walk
- 30-45s pec stretch each side
- Debrief: protect grip early so the buy-out stays possible; runs stay conversational and together
