**Setup**

- **Equipment**: barbell + plates (hang clean & jerk + squat cleans), calorie machine (rower/bike/ski)
- **Timing**: 60-min class cap; hang C&J ~12 min (E90s × 8); metcon 20 min (5× 2:00 on / 2:00 off)
