# Tuesday (28/07/2026)

**Setup**

- **Equipment**: rack + barbell + plates (push press), KBs 24/16, boxes 60/50, wall balls 10/6 or plates 10/5 for sit-ups
- **Timing**: 60-min class cap; 1RM push press ~15-18 min; team AMRAP 20 min
- **Stimulus**: heavy overhead strength from the rack, then partner I-go-you-go pacing with synchro sit-ups every round
- **Scaling A**: push press to a tough single (not absolute max); PP 40/20; KB 20/12; step-ups; keep synchro sit-ups
- **Scaling B**: push press build to a solid triple; DB push press; Russian swings; box step-ups; sit-ups without load
- **Logistics**: claim racks for 1RM work; one bar + KB + box per team; brief synchro sit-up standard before the clock
- **Top Cues**:
  - **Push press**: “Dip straight, punch up.”
  - **KB swing**: “Snap hips, float the bell.”
  - **Synchro sit-ups**: “Shoulders touch together.”

  - 6 down-ups

- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard)**: 2 rounds:
  - 4 push press (empty bar)
  - 4 box step-ups / low jumps

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)

- 30-45s overhead/lat stretch each side
- 30-45s wrist extension stretch

## Strength / Skill

### A) Find 1RM — Push Press

Start from the **rack**. Build to a 1RM.

Suggested ladder (adjust to athlete): empty → ~50% → 70% → 80% → 90% → singles.

**Time budget: ~15-18 min**

## Reset Window

3-4 min to set partner stations (PP load 50/25, KB 24/16, box 60/50, sit-up implement).

## Movement Prep

1 short partner practice round:

- 4 push press @ workout load
- 4 KB swings @ workout load
- 4 box jumps (or step-ups)
- 6 synchro sit-ups (with ball/plate)

## Main Conditioning

### B) Team of 2 — 20' AMRAP

**Standard**: **I-go-you-go by exercise** (one partner completes the movement, then the other).

Each round:

- 10 **Push Press** (50/25 kg)
- 10 **KB Swings** (24/16 kg)
- 10 **Box Jumps** (60/50 cm)

**Every round**: **15 synchro sit-ups** (wall ball 10/6 or plate 10/5)

**Expected rounds: 4-7 (steady handoffs + unbroken synchro sit-ups)**

**Scaling options**

- **Scaling A**: PP 40/20, KB 20/12, step-ups ok, keep synchro sit-ups
- **Scaling B**: DB push press, Russian swings, low box/step-ups, unweighted sit-ups (still synchro)

## Cooldown & Debrief

- 60-90s easy walk + breathing
- 30-45s pec stretch each side
- Quick debrief: clean handoffs; sit-ups stay synchronized every round
