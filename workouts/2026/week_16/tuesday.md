# Tuesday (14/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Kettlebells — one heavy bell for 1RM TGU building,
  one pair at 24/16 kg for the main block
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Find a true 1RM Turkish get-up first, then cycle
  through a single-arm KB complex at a controlled pace.
- Scaling A: TGU at 20/12 kg. KB swings to 24/16. Press at
  same load as TGU.
- Scaling B: TGU with non-dominant side guided by coach.
  Reduce swings to 15 reps. Press seated on box if mobility
  limits overhead.
- Logistics: Stage one heavy bell and one conditioning bell
  side-by-side per athlete. Keep the floor clear for get-up
  before the strength block.
- Top Cues: "Press the floor away on the way up."
  "Hinge, don't swing the shoulder." "Tall wrist on the press."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Hip circles (each direction)
  - 8 x Shoulder CARs (each arm)
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Single-arm carries — 20m
  per arm, two passes
  - 5 x Bird-dogs per side
  - 5 x Half-kneeling thoracic rotations per side
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 3 x 10s hard KB swing burst,
  easy walk between
  - 3 x TGU get-up to elbow (light bell or bodyweight)
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Thoracic extension over foam roller or rig: 45s
- Hip flexor / lunge stretch: 45s/side

## Skill: Find 1RM TGU (10 min, Tc: 10')
- Build from a comfortable warm-up weight in 3-4 jumps.
- Test both sides. Score is the heavier successful rep.
- Prioritise perfect lockout and a controlled descent over
  max load.

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Drop to conditioning bell (24/16 kg). Mark a clear floor
  lane. Confirm pressing standard and switching cues.

## Movement Prep (2 min)
- 1 x TGU @ 24/16 each side
- 10 x KB swings @ 24/16
- 5 x One-arm KB press R + 5 L @ conditioning load

## Main Conditioning: E3MOM (18 min)
**Every 3:00 for 6 sets:**

- 2 TGU @ 24/16 kg (1 per side)
- 20 KB Swings @ 24/16 kg
- 10 One-Arm KB Press — Right
- 10 One-Arm KB Press — Left

Target: complete each set with 0-30s remaining. Scale TGU
load or reduce swings to 15 if consistently missing the 3-min
window.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Thread-the-needle thoracic stretch: 30s/side
3. Wrist extension rocks: 30s
4. Bright spot: "I kept the TGU transitions smooth even when
   the bell felt heavy."
