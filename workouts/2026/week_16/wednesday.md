# Wednesday (15/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates, rope (climbing rope or rope
  ring alternative)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Progressive snatch EMOM through four technique
  focuses, then a short AMRAP that turns snatch mechanics
  into conditioning work.
- Scaling A: Power snatch + overhead squat in place of squat
  snatch. Rope climb to 1 legless or 2 rope-to-standing rows.
- Scaling B: Hang power snatch throughout the EMOM.
  Conditioning load to 35/20 kg. Rope climb to 2 ring rows
  to standing.
- Logistics: Keep the conditioning bar loaded separately from
  the EMOM bar. One rope per lane or stagger start by 15s.
- Top Cues: "Stay over the bar below the knee."
  "Punch up into the catch." "Set the feet before the pull."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog or PVC carry walk
  - 8 x PVC pass-throughs
  - 8 x Squat-to-stand
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Jog with 5s build each
  length
  - 5 x PVC snatch-grip good mornings + press
  - 5 x PVC overhead squats
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 20m hard sprint,
  walk back
  - 3 x Squat jumps
  - 3 x PVC snatch balance
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Ankle rocks in deep squat: 45s

## Skill: 12' EMOM — Squat Snatch Wave
**Minutes 1–3:**
- 2 Squat Snatches @ 67-72% (2s pause below knees)

**Minutes 4–6:**
- 2 Squat Snatches @ 72-77% (2s pause in the catch)

**Minutes 7–9:**
- 2 Hang Squat Snatches @ 85%

**Minutes 10–12:**
- 1 Squat Snatch @ 90-95% + 1 Overhead Squat

Load note: each 3-minute block uses a single weight.
Increase by feel — positions, not a number.

## Reset Window (4 min)
- Drop to conditioning load. Hang the rope and confirm
  the standard. Rehearse one rope climb or scaled version.

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 3 x Deadlift (snatch grip) @ workout load
- 3 x Hang squat snatches @ workout load
- 1 x Rope climb

## Main Conditioning: 10' AMRAP
- 10 Deadlifts — snatch grip (50/30 kg)
- 5 Hang Squat Snatches
- 1 Rope Climb

**Expected rounds:** 5-7 (fit: 6-7, ~85-100s/round;
average: 4-5, ~105-130s/round)

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Pigeon (deep glute): 45s/side
3. Puppy dog pose: 45s
4. Bright spot: "I kept the snatch positions under AMRAP pace."
