# Thursday (16/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rowing machine, open run lane or 200m loop,
  Assault Air Bike (AAB), KB or plate 10/5 kg for accessory
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Pure aerobic endurance — three 8-minute team
  AMRAPs, each on a different machine/modality, with short
  switches to keep both athletes working and breathing hard.
- Scaling A: Shorten run distance to 150m per switch if space
  is limited. Row and bike distances unchanged.
- Scaling B: Row 200m per switch. Run 100m or 3 laps of a
  shorter loop. Bike 400m per switch.
- Logistics: Team of 2 shares one machine per station. Stage
  machines before class. 2-minute rest between stations is
  used to walk to the next machine and catch breath.
- Top Cues: "Hold a pace you can sustain for 8 minutes."
  "Hand off cleanly — no wasted seconds at the switch."
  "Breathe through the rest, not after the round."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy row or jog — full breathing
  - 8 x Leg swings per side
  - 8 x Arm circles per side
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Easy bike or moderate jog
  - 10 x Hip circles per side
  - 10 x Shoulder rolls
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s hard surge on
  the day's first machine, easy spin between
  - 4 x High knees
  - 4 x Butt kicks
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Hip flexor / lunge stretch: 45s/side
- Calf and ankle stretch: 45s/side

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — Endurance Stations (30 min)
**3 x 8' AMRAP / Rest 2' between stations:**

**Station A — Max Meters Row**
- Partners switch every 250m.

**Station B — Max Meters Run**
- Partners switch every 200m (approx. 3 laps on a short loop).

**Station C — Max Meters Assault Air Bike**
- Partners switch every 500m.

Note: The goal is maximum total distance at each station.
Agree on a switch signal before each AMRAP starts.

## Accessory (5 min)
**Choose one:**
- 5-minute Plank (accumulated; break only as needed), OR
- 300 Russian Twists (10/5 kg) for time

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 90s easy walk or bike, nasal breathing — full
   parasympathetic downshift
2. Pigeon (deep glute): 45s/side
3. Child's pose: 45s
4. Bright spot: "We held consistent split times across all
   three stations."
