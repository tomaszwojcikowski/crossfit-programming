# Friday (17/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Box (60/50 cm), pull-up rig (for BMU and T2B),
  barbell, plates (60/35 kg)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Long team grinder cycling through three couplets
  in I-go-you-go format. Each block hits a different demand:
  power + gymnastics, core + barbell, breathing + legs.
- Scaling A: BMU to 2 chest-to-bar + 2 ring dips each rep.
  T2B to hanging knee raises. Box to 50/40 cm.
- Scaling B: BMU to jumping muscle-ups or banded muscle-ups.
  T2B to kipping knee raises. Load to 50/25 kg. Burpee
  over bar to down-up over bar.
- Logistics: One bar and one box per team, close to the rig.
  Agree on round-switching cues before the clock starts.
  Cycle through the 3 blocks in order; move to the next
  block after completing 4 rounds of the current one.
- Top Cues: "Full extension on the box jump."
  "Pull the bar down fast on the muscle-up."
  "Stay hollow through the thruster drive."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Push-ups
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Line hops — side-to-side
  and front-to-back
  - 4 x Down-dog to pike push-up
  - 6 x Hip hinges with reach
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 3 x 5s fast box jump
  singles (sub-maximal height, full hip extension)
  - 2 x Wall kick-ups to heel touch
  - 3 x Kip swings + transition practice
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Wrist extension rocks: 30s
- Lat stretch on rig: 30s/side

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 2 x Box jumps (workout height)
- 2 x BMU or scaled version
- 3 x T2B
- 2 x Empty-bar clean & jerk
- 3 x Empty-bar thrusters
- 2 x Burpee over the bar
- Confirm switch standard and transitions before the clock

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 26' AMRAP
**Cycle through blocks in order, I go–You go full round.**

**Block 1 — 4 rounds:**
- 10 Box Jumps (60/50 cm)
- 5 Bar Muscle-Ups

**Block 2 — 4 rounds:**
- 10 Toes-to-Bar
- 5 Clean & Jerks (60/35 kg)

**Block 3 — 4 rounds:**
- 10 Burpees Over the Bar
- 5 Thrusters (60/35 kg)

**Expected rounds (individual I-go efforts):**
Fit teams: clear blocks 1 and 2, get 2-4 rounds into block 3
(~18-20 individual rounds).
Average teams: clear block 1, reach 5-7 rounds into block 2
(~12-15 individual rounds).

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Child's pose: 45s
3. Hamstring fold: 45s
4. Bright spot: "We kept handoffs clean and didn't let the
   muscle-ups break our rhythm in block 1."
