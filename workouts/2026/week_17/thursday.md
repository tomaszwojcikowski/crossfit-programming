# Thursday (23/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, open wall space, 10 m handstand walk
  lane, sandbag or D-ball 50/30 kg, dumbbell 22.5/15 kg
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Long gymnastics EMOM with trunk and carry support.
  Minutes 1-5 stay honest; minute 6 is protected rest.
- Scaling A: HSW -> wall taps x10-16. T2B -> hanging knee
  raises. Bearhug carry lighter but keep 45s. DB snatches at
  15/10 kg.
- Scaling B: Bear crawl 2 x 5 m instead of HSW. V-ups or strict
  knee raises instead of T2B. Plate bearhug carry. DB snatches
  at 10/7.5 kg.
- Logistics: Assign one lane per athlete for wall, rig, and
  carry path. Minute 6 remains full rest so athletes can return
  to their start point before the next round.
- Top Cues: "Stack before you walk." "Kick toes to the bar."
  "Stay tall on the carry."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Jog or shuttle runs
  - 8 x Scap pull-ups
  - 8 x Hollow rocks
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Line hops or quick-feet
  shuffle
  - 6 x Push-up to down-dog
  - 6 x Alternating dead bugs/side
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s acceleration or fast
  bear crawl bursts
  - 2 x Wall walk steps or kick-ups
  - 4 x Hanging knee raises
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 30s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 30s

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 2 x 5 m HSW or scale
- 4 x T2B or hanging knee raises
- 15s bearhug hold + 10 m walk
- 6 x Alternating DB snatches

## Main Conditioning: 30' EMOM
**Every minute on the minute for 30 minutes (5 rounds):**

- **Minute 1:** 2 x 5 m Handstand Walk
- **Minute 2:** 12 Toes-to-Bar
- **Minute 3:** 45s Bearhug Farmer Carry (50/30 kg)
- **Minute 4:** 45s Plank
- **Minute 5:** Max Alternating DB Snatches (22.5/15 kg)
- **Minute 6:** Rest

**Target:** Minutes 1-2 finish in ~25-40s, minutes 3-4 are a
true 45s of work, and minute 5 should still leave ~10-20s to
reset before the rest minute.

## Cooldown & Debrief (4 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Puppy pose (shoulders): 45s
3. Forearm stretch: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "I protected the rest minute instead of chasing
   sloppy snatches."
