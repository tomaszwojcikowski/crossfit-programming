# Friday (24/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates (100/65 kg), wall ball, kettlebell
  24/16 kg, open bar-facing lane
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team progression from a fast deadlift-burpee sprint
  into a simple wall ball and KB rhythm block, then one longer
  mixed-modal AMRAP that rewards smart splitting.
- Scaling A: Deadlift 80/55 kg. Burpee over bar to down-up over
  bar. Wall ball lighter. KB swings at 20/12 kg.
- Scaling B: Deadlift 60/40 kg. Line hops instead of bar
  transitions. Wall ball to thruster with light DB. Russian KB
  swings lighter.
- Logistics: One lane per team with bar, wall ball, and KB all
  staged together. One athlete works at a time unless the team
  chooses quick handoffs within a movement.
- Top Cues: "Fast hips on the deadlift." "Land and move right
  away." "Split before grip fades."
