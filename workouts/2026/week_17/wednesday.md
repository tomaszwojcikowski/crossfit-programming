# Wednesday (22/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Air Runner or run lane, rower, open floor space
  for down-ups, Assault Air Bike, ski erg
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team aerobic density across five stations repeated
  twice. Frequent switches keep output high without turning the
  piece into a sprint in interval one.
- Scaling A: Air Runner switch every 100 m. Row every 125 m.
  AAB every 200 m. Ski every 100 m. Down-ups unchanged.
- Scaling B: Replace Air Runner and ski with 60-80 m shuttle
  runs if machines are limited. Row every 100 m. Down-ups to
  sprawl-to-stand.
- Logistics: Build fixed team pods and rotate A -> E twice on a
  single running clock. Teams switch when the working athlete
  completes the listed distance or reps.
- Top Cues: "Settle into round one, then build." "Switch early,
  not late." "Win the handoff."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy machine flush or jog
  - 8 x Leg swings/side
  - 8 x Arm circles/side
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Moderate machine pace or
  shuttle rhythm
  - 6 x Down-ups
  - 6 x Alternating lunges
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s hard effort on the
  day's first station, easy spin or walk between
  - 4 x Fast feet to sprawl
  - 4 x Broad jumps
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (60-90s)
- Hip flexor stretch: 45s/side
- Calf stretch on rig or wall: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 1 x Air Runner or shuttle build to switch line
- 150 m easy row
- 3 x Down-ups
- 125 m moderate ski

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 10 x 3' AMRAP / 1' Rest

**Rotate through the 5 stations twice:**

- **A:** Max meters Air Runner, switch every 125 m
- **B:** Max meters Row, switch every 150 m
- **C:** Max Down-Ups, switch every 5 reps
- **D:** Max meters Assault Air Bike, switch every 250 m
- **E:** Max meters Ski, switch every 125 m

Use the 1-minute rest to rotate, log the score, and get onto
the next station. Goal: repeatable hard aerobic output, not an
all-out opening interval.

**Target:** 5-8 switches each work interval. Hold a sustainable
hard pace for 2 minutes, then push the final minute.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk or spin, nasal breathing
2. Quad stretch: 30s/side
3. Bright spot: "Our switches stayed clean and the second round
   looked like the first."
