# Monday (20/04/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Barbell, plates, jump rope, open wall space for
  wall walks
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Technical clean-and-jerk volume first, then a short
  mixed-modal AMRAP where barbell cycling stays crisp under
  inversion and rope fatigue.
- Scaling A: 50/30 kg on the AMRAP. Wall walks 3 -> 2. Double
  unders 20 or 40 single unders.
- Scaling B: Power clean + front squat + push jerk in the
  strength block. AMRAP at 40/25 kg. Inchworm to wall tap or
  box pike walk instead of wall walks.
- Logistics: One bar and one wall lane per athlete. Stage the
  rope beside the bar before the strength block starts so the
  whole class can move straight into the AMRAP.
- Top Cues: "Stand tall before the jerk." "Punch under the
  bar." "Keep the rope light and relaxed."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy shuttle jog or jump rope
  bounce
  - 8 x Bootstrappers
  - 8 x Scap push-ups
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Lateral line hops or rope
  rhythm build
  - 6 x Front rack elbow lifts
  - 6 x Hollow body rocks
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 3 x 10s fast rope cadence or
  shuttle acceleration, easy walk between
  - 3 x Jump-and-stick
  - 2 x Inchworms to plank
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Front rack stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Wrist extension rocks: 45s

## Strength / Skill (16 min)
**Every 2:00 for 8 sets:**

- 2 Power Cleans
- 2 Front Squats
- 2 Jerks

Build across the 8 sets to a heavy technical load. Prioritise
footwork and vertical dip-drive over chasing a miss.

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Keep the bar loaded for the metcon. Mark wall-walk lanes and
  confirm double-under scale before the clock starts.

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 1 x Squat clean + jerk @ workout load
- 1 x Wall walk or scaled version
- 10 x Double unders or 20 x single unders

## Main Conditioning: 12' AMRAP

- 6 Squat Clean & Jerks (60/35 kg)
- 3 Wall Walks
- 30 Double Unders

**Expected rounds:** 3-5 (fit: 4-5+, ~2:15-2:45/round;
average: 3-4, ~2:45-3:45/round)

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Puppy pose (lats/shoulders): 45s
3. Bright spot: "I kept the bar path clean even when the rope
   and wall walks raised the heart rate."
