# Thursday (14/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Dumbbells (22.5/15 kg), clear overhead lunge lane, wall for strict HSPU and HSW, open floor for bear crawl, kettlebell or dumbbell 24/16 kg
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Three short skill-sprint AMRAPs repeated twice. Each 3-minute block should feel fast but controlled, with the 90-second rests used to reset before the next demand.
- Scaling A: Devil press to 2x15/10 kg. OH lunges to front-rack lunges. Strict HSPU to 6 pike push-ups. Pistols to alternating box step-downs. HSW 5 m to 2 wall walks. Goblet squat to 20/12 kg.
- Scaling B: Single-DB devil press. Reverse lunges instead of OH lunges. Hand-release push-ups instead of HSPU. Assisted pistols. Bear crawl in place instead of HSW. Sit-ups instead of one-leg V-ups.
- Logistics: Set three lanes before class. Keep athletes in the same lane for both cycles so rest transitions stay under 15s.
- Top Cues: "Lock the ribcage before you lunge." "Press the floor away." "Move fast, but keep each rep clean."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, shuttle runs, or line hops
  - 6 x Alternating reverse lunges
  - 20s Hollow hold
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Fast feet in place or low-amplitude bounce
  - 4 x Inchworms
  - 4 x Goblet squats light
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s quick turnover line hops
  - 2 x Devil presses light
  - 10m Bear crawl or 1 wall walk
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Wrist extension rocks: 30s
- Hip flexor stretch: 30s/side

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 2 x Devil presses @ workout load
- 4 x Overhead lunges total
- 2 x Strict HSPU or scaled version
- 4 x Alternating pistols or scale
- 5m HSW or 10m bear crawl
- 3 x Goblet squats

## Main Conditioning: 2 Cycles
**Cycle 1**
- **A) 3' AMRAP**
  - 5 Devil Press (2x22.5/15 kg)
  - 10 Overhead Lunges
  - 15 Butterfly Sit-Ups
- **Rest 1:30**
- **B) 3' AMRAP**
  - 5 Strict HSPU
  - 10 Alternating Pistols
  - 15 Alternating One-Leg V-Ups
- **Rest 1:30**
- **C) 3' AMRAP**
  - 5m Handstand Walk or 15m Bear Crawl
  - 10 Goblet Squats (24/16 kg)
  - 15 Crunches
- **Rest 1:30**

**Repeat A, B, and C one more time.**

Score each AMRAP separately.

**Target:** expect 2-4 rounds per 3-minute block depending on the gymnastic skill. Use the rest to restore positions, not to wander between lanes.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Puppy pose: 45s
3. Figure-4 stretch: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "Which lane stayed most repeatable in both cycles?"
