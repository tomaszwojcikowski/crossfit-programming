# Tuesday (12/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Jump ropes, rope climb station, wall space, pull-up rig, 2 kettlebells (24/16 kg), box (75/60 cm)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Repeated 2-minute intervals alternating a gymnastics-heavy piece with a power-and-cyclical piece. Athletes should attack each window, then use the full 2-minute rest to recover.
- Scaling A: 40 single-unders instead of 20 double-unders. Rope climb to 2 rope pulls from floor. Wall walks 3 to 2. Toes-to-bar to hanging knee raises. Dual KB snatches at 20/12 kg. Box to 60/50 cm.
- Scaling B: 30 line hops instead of rope. Inchworm to wall tap instead of wall walks. Russian KB swings instead of dual KB snatches. Step-ups instead of box jumps.
- Logistics: Divide the room into Interval A lanes and Interval B lanes before class. Athletes should not carry KBs across the rope/wall area between windows.
- Top Cues: "Stay long through the rope pull." "Snap the bells overhead." "Land soft, stand tall."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy shuttle jog or rope skip rhythm
  - 20s Hollow hold
  - 6 x Scap pull-ups
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Double-under timing practice or pogo hops
  - 2 x Inchworms
  - 4 x Alternating single-arm KB high pulls/side
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast rope cadence
  - 1 x Wall walk or 4 wall-walk steps
  - 2 x Dual KB snatches
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Calf stretch on wall: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (4 min)
- 10 x Double-unders or 20 x single-unders
- 1 x Rope climb or 2 rope pulls
- 1 x Wall walk
- 3 x Toes-to-bar or 4 x knee raises
- 4 x Dual KB snatches
- 2 x Box jumps

## Main Conditioning: Intervals
**8 x 2:00 ON / 2:00 OFF, alternating A and B (4 rounds each):**

- **A)** 2' AMRAP
  - 20 Double-Unders
  - 1 Rope Climb
  - 3 Wall Walks
  - 5 Toes-to-Bar
- **B)** 2' AMRAP
  - 20 Double-Unders
  - 10 Dual KB Snatches (2x24/16 kg)
  - 5 Box Jumps (75/60 cm)

Score each interval as rounds + reps.

**Target:** A should be about 1 round plus a few reps for most athletes. B should allow 1-2 fast rounds if the KBs stay unbroken.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Puppy pose: 45s
3. Forearm stretch: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "Which interval let you recover faster, and why?"
