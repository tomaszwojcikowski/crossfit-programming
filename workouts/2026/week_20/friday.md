# Friday (15/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Pull-up rig, barbell, plates (60/35, 80/45, 90/55 kg)
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Team-of-2 barbell and gymnastics window. The first two blocks are fixed work; the last block is a cash-out AMRAP if teams manage the early pacing well.
- Scaling A: Pull-ups to chest-to-bar or banded pull-ups based on capacity. BMU to 2 chest-to-bar + 2 ring dips. Cleans to 70/45 kg.
- Scaling B: Jumping pull-ups or ring rows. BMU to jumping muscle-ups or burpee pull-ups. Deadlift loads reduced by 10-20 kg. Cleans to 60/35 kg.
- Logistics: One team per rig bay and bar. Stage the plates in change order before the warmup. Partners alternate full rounds or full complexes from the start.
- Top Cues: "Stay close on every clean." "Switch fast, not frantic." "Own the pull before chasing the muscle-up."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, row, or shuttle runs
  - 6 x Scap pull-ups
  - 8 x Hip hinges
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Quick line hops or rope-skip rhythm
  - 4 x Empty-bar deadlifts
  - 4 x Empty-bar front squats
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** 2 x 15s fast pull-up kip swing or high-cadence shuttle burst
  - 2 x Empty-bar hang cleans
  - 1 x Empty-bar split jerk
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Lat stretch on rig: 45s/side
- Front rack stretch: 45s/side

## Movement Prep (5 min)
- 1 x Complex at empty bar:
  - 5 Deadlifts
  - 4 Hang Cleans
  - 3 Front Squats
  - 2 Jerks
- 3 x Pull-ups or scaled version
- 1 x Bar muscle-up transition drill or scaled option
- 2 x Power cleans @ final-block load

## Main Conditioning: Team of 2 — 25' Window
**Partners alternate full rounds / full complexes for the entire workout.**

- **Part 1:** 10 total complexes
  - 5 Deadlifts
  - 4 Hang Cleans
  - 3 Front Squats
  - 2 Jerks
  - Load: 60/35 kg
- **Part 2:** 10 total rounds
  - 10 Pull-Ups
  - 5 Deadlifts (80/45 kg)
- **Into remaining time:**
  - 3 Bar Muscle-Ups
  - 3 Cleans (90/55 kg)

**Expected flow:** Part 1 ~6-9 min, Part 2 ~8-11 min, leaving ~5-10 min for the final cash-out AMRAP.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Forearm stretch: 30s/side
3. Child's pose: 45s
4. Bright spot: "Our handoffs stayed clean enough to buy extra time for the BMU block."
