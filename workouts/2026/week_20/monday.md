# Monday (11/05/2026)

**Setup**
- Equipment: Rack, barbell, plates, open burpee-over-bar lane
- Timing: 60 min hard cap
- Stimulus: Lower-body strength first, then overhead strength, then a fast barbell complex EMOM that rewards crisp cycling and clean footwork over the bar.
- Scaling A: Back squat and push press at 90-95% of the prescribed working load for the day. Complex starts at 50/30 kg.
- Scaling B: Back squat at RPE 7-8 across. Strict press instead of push press if dip-drive timing is not consistent. Complex starts at 40/25 kg with 2 down-ups over the bar.
- Logistics: One bar and rack per athlete. Keep the lane beside the rack clear from the start so the class can move straight into the EMOM.
- Top Cues: "Drive the floor away." "Punch the bar to lockout." "Jump over the bar, not around it."

## Warmup (10 min)
- **R1 RAISE (2:00 easy):** Easy jog, shuttle runs, or jumping jacks
  - 8 x Glute bridges
  - 8 x Air squats
- **R2 ACTIVATE (1:30 moderate):** Fast feet over a floor line
  - 6 x Empty-bar good mornings
  - 6 x Front rack elbow lifts
- **R3 POTENTIATE (1:00 hard):** Short acceleration shuttles or 2 x 15s quick pogo hops
  - 3 x Empty-bar push press
  - 2 x Burpee pop-ups over the bar
- Rest 30s between rounds

## Post-Warmup Mobility (90s)
- Couch stretch: 45s/side
- Front rack stretch on rig: 45s/side

## Strength A: Back Squat (15 min)
**Every 3:00 for 5 sets:**
- 5 Back Squats

Source note preserved: the board shows **5x5 @ 90%**. Treat this as a heavy working 5 across at roughly RPE 8-9, not 90% of 1RM.

## Strength B: Push Press (10 min)
**Every 2:00 for 5 sets:**
- 5 Push Press
- Build across the sets to a heavy technical set of 5

## Reset Window (3 min)
- Strip the bar to complex load. Mark the burpee-over-bar lane. Take one rehearsal round with an empty bar.

## Movement Prep (3 min)
- 1 x Power clean + 1 x Hang squat clean + 1 x Jerk with empty bar
- 2 x Burpees over the bar
- 1 x Full complex @ starting load

## Main Conditioning: Complex EMOM (8 min)
**Every 1:00 for 8 sets:**
- 1 Power Clean
- 1 Hang Squat Clean
- 1 Jerk
- 3 Burpees Over the Bar

Start at **60/35 kg** and stay there or build only if every rep stays sharp.

**Target:** finish each minute in ~35-45s and keep 15-25s to reset before the next set.

## Cooldown & Debrief (3 min)
1. 60-90s easy walk, nasal breathing
2. Pigeon stretch: 45s/side
3. Wrist extension stretch: 30s/side
4. Bright spot: "The complex stayed crisp because I never rushed the clean-to-jerk transition."
